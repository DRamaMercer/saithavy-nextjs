### 4. Ritual Troubleshooting Guide
**File:** `ritual-troubleshooting-guide.md`

**What It Is:**
A comprehensive guide to diagnosing and fixing common ritual problems before they sink your culture.

**Covers 8 Common Issues:**

1. **People Show Up But Don't Participate**
   - Diagnosis: Safety issue or bad format
   - Fixes: Anonymous input, smaller groups, rotate facilitators, check format
   - Real example: From crickets to floodgates

2. **Feels Like "Just Another Meeting"**
   - Diagnosis: Ritual drift
   - Fixes: Reconnect to purpose, refresh format, shorten it, add play
   - Real example: 2-minute headline that transformed energy

3. **We Keep Skipping It**
   - Diagnosis: Consistency problem
   - Fixes: Lock the time, assign ownership, backup plan, lower the bar
   - Real example: 15-minute learning time vs. canceling

4. **It Worked at First, But Now It's Stale**
   - Diagnosis: Ritual fatigue
   - Fixes: Seasonal refreshes, team-led redesign, take a break, merge/split
   - Real example: Appreciation Bingo for fresh energy

5. **New Team Members Resist**
   - Diagnosis: Onboarding gap
   - Fixes: Explain history, assign buddy, opt-in at first, create docs
   - Real example: Context converts skeptic to advocate

6. **It Takes Too Long**
   - Diagnosis: Scope creep
   - Fixes: Cut in half, split it, tighten format, reduce participants
   - Real example: 60→30 minutes with same results

7. **Energy Is Consistently Low**
   - Diagnosis: Purpose/vision disconnect
   - Fixes: Reconnect to purpose, check team health, get input, retire it
   - Real example: Replaced celebration with support check

8. **Works for Some, Not Others**
   - Diagnosis: One-size-fits-all problem
   - Fixes: Multiple formats, flexible participation, balance types, ask for input
   - Real example: Remote-first retro for equal participation

**Includes:**
- Quick diagnosis table
- Step-by-step fixes for each issue
- Real examples from teams I've led
- Ritual health check (quarterly assessment tool)

## Key Principles

### 1. Less Is More
Start with 3-4 rituals total, not 10. Quality over quantity.

### 2. Consistency Is Everything
Same time, every week/month. Sporadic rituals are worse than no rituals.

### 3. Your Presence Matters
Model the energy you want to see. Your presence is your primary leadership tool.

### 4. Build Strength Through the Reps
The first week will be awkward. The fourth week will feel natural. Trust the process.

### 5. Iterate Often
Review rituals quarterly. Keep what works, change what doesn't, retire what's stale.
