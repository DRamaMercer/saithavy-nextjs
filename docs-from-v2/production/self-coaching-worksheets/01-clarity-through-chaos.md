# Worksheet 1: Clarity Through Chaos
## When You Feel Stuck or Overwhelmed

---

### PURPOSE
This worksheet helps you cut through mental fog and emotional overwhelm when you feel stuck, paralyzed, or unclear about your next steps. It uses the **Rebuild Formula**: Truth → Reframe → Practice → Anchor.

**Time to complete:** 20-30 minutes
**Best used when:** You're procrastinating, avoiding decisions, or feeling paralyzed by too many options

---

### PERSONAL NOTE FROM THE CREATOR

I created this worksheet during a season when I was juggling a business pivot, a cross-country move, and questioning whether I was on the right path at all. I spent three weeks paralyzed by "analysis paralysis" - cycling through the same thoughts without making any decisions.

What broke the cycle? Admitting I was scared. Not "busy," not "strategic" - scared.

Once I named that truth, everything shifted. I stopped trying to solve everything at once and started taking messy, imperfect action. One small step. Then another. Then another.

This worksheet is what I wish I'd had then. It's the process that got me unstuck - and continues to, every time chaos returns.

**Remember:** Feeling stuck doesn't mean you're broken. It usually means you're growing.

You're allowed to start small. Just don't stay unclear.

### QUICK REFERENCE: CLARITY TRIGGERS

**Common chaos triggers:**
- Too many options → **Narrow to 3 choices max**
- Fear of failure → **Reframe as learning**
- Perfectionism → **"Progress over perfection"**
- Exhaustion → **Rest is productive**
- Comparison → **Stay in your lane**

**Emergency clarity protocol (5 minutes):**
1. Name what's swirling (write it down)
2. Identify what you can control
3. Choose ONE small action
4. Take it before you second-guess
