## **📅 Day 1: Critical Fixes (2 Hours)**
*Focus: Prevent immediate injury and strain*

### **Physical Setup**
- [ ] Monitor positioned at eye level (top of screen at or below eye level)
- [ ] Monitor positioned at arm's length distance (20-26 inches)
- [ ] Desk height allows elbows at 90° when typing
- [ ] Chair positioned to support lumbar (fills gap behind lower back)
- [ ] Feet positioned flat on floor or on footrest
- [ ] Shoulders relaxed (not hunched up)
- [ ] Wrists in neutral position (not bent up/down)
- [ ] Monitor positioned directly in front (no neck twisting)
- [ ] Keyboard and mouse centered (no reaching)
- [ ] Movement break timer set (every 30-60 minutes)

- [ ] Can sit comfortably for 1 hour without pain
