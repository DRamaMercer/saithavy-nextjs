### Step 2: Document Platform DNA Profiles
**Time Required:** 3-4 hours | **Complexity:** Low | **ROI:** High

Create a platform specification document for each platform:

```markdown
# TikTok Platform DNA Profile

## Technical Specifications
- **Format:** 9:16 vertical video
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 minimum (720p acceptable)
- **Duration:** 15-90 seconds (optimal: 21-34s)
- **FPS:** 30-60
- **File Size:** < 287MB

## Algorithm Signals (Priority Order)
1. **Watch time** - Total completion rate
2. **Rewatch rate** - Repeat views indicator
3. **Shares** - Viral coefficient
4. **Comments** - Engagement depth
5. **Likes** - Social proof (lowest value)

## Psychological Hooks
### Visual
- **Pattern interrupt:** Violate expectation in first 0.5s
- **Color psychology:** High contrast warm colors (red/orange)
- **Motion:** Camera movement every 2-3 seconds

### Audio
- **Trending sounds:** Must use within 24h of trending
- **Sonic branding:** 0.5-1s audio signature at end
- **Binaural beats:** Optional 40Hz gamma for focus

### Narrative
- **Hook structure:** Hook (0-3s) → Value (3-15s) → CTA (15-21s)
- **Curiosity gap:** Open loop in first 1 second
- **Social proof:** Show results before process

## Content Patterns That Work
- Green screen reactions
- Stitch responses
- POV storytelling
- Duet chains
- Behind-the-scenes

## Hashtag Strategy
- 3-5 hashtags maximum
- Mix of trending (1M+) and niche (10K-100K)
- Always include location tag
- Use niche-specific hashtags

## Optimal Posting Windows
- **Morning:** 7-9 AM (commute)
- **Lunch:** 12-2 PM (break)
- **Evening:** 7-10 PM (prime time)
- **Late night:** 11 PM-1 AM (insomniacs)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Low resolution or poor lighting
- ❌ No hook in first 3 seconds
- ❌ Overly produced/corporate feel
- ❌ Ignoring trending sounds
- ❌ Not using location tags
- ❌ Too many hashtags
- ❌ No call-to-action
```

**Platforms to document:**
1. TikTok ✓
2. Instagram (Reels, Stories, Feed)
3. YouTube (Long-form, Shorts)
4. Twitter/X
5. LinkedIn
6. Pinterest
7. Facebook

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Research Workflow
1. **Set up Google Alerts** for: "TikTok algorithm", "Instagram update", "YouTube algorithm", "Twitter algorithm", "LinkedIn algorithm"
2. **Follow platform blogs** for official announcements
3. **Join platform communities** for real-time insights
4. **Use social listening** to track trends early

### Content Atomization
1. **Start with long-form** (YouTube or podcast)
2. **Extract viral moments** (high engagement segments)
3. **Adapt natively** per platform (don't cross-post)
4. **Track lineage** (parent → child relationships)
5. **Measure attribution** (which derivative drives best ROI)

### GEO Optimization
1. **Authoritative tone:** Use confident language
2. **Statistics:** Add data points and percentages
3. **Citations:** Link to authoritative sources
4. **Structured formatting:** Use lists and schema markup
5. **Easy to understand:** Simplify language

### Psychological Hooks
1. **Pattern interrupt:** Violate expectations early
2. **Curiosity gap:** Withhold resolution
3. **Social proof:** Show numbers/results
4. **Authority:** Signal expertise immediately
5. **Emotional arousal:** High energy opening
