# Hands-On Exercises

## Exercise 1: First Content Creation (Day 3)

### Objective
Create your first piece of content for your chosen platform.

### Requirements
1. Choose a trending topic or theme
2. Create a 15-60 second video or an image post
3. Write a compelling caption with hashtags
4. Post during peak time
5. Monitor engagement for 24 hours

### Deliverables
- Screenshot of your post
- Engagement metrics after 24 hours
- 3 lessons learned
- 3 improvements for next time

### Duration
1 hour for creation, 24 hours for monitoring

### Evaluation
- Content quality (20 points)
- Caption effectiveness (20 points)
- Hashtag relevance (20 points)
- Engagement results (20 points)
- Reflection quality (20 points)

**Total: 100 points, Passing: 70 points**
