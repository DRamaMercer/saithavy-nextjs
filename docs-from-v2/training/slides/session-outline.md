### Slide 1: Title Slide

---

### Slide 2: TikTok Algorithm Deep Dive

**Key Ranking Factors:**
1. **Watch Time** - Most important metric
2. **Completion Rate** - Do they watch to the end?
3. **Engagement Velocity** - Speed of engagement
4. **Shares** - Viral catalyst
5. **Saves** - Value indicator

**The 3-Second Rule:** Hook them or lose them

---

### Slide 3: Content Format Mastery

**Optimal Video Length:** 15-60 seconds
**Dimensions:** 1080x1920 (9:16)

**Content Types:**
- Dance challenges (viral potential)
- Educational tutorials (high saves)
- Behind-the-scenes (authenticity)
- Trend participation (algorithm boost)
- Storytelling (engagement)

---

### Slide 4: Trending Audio Strategy

**Why Trending Audio Matters:**
- Algorithm boost
- Higher completion rates
- Increased discoverability

**Strategy:**
- Use trending sounds (check Creative Center)
- Add unique spin to trends
- Don't force trend fit
- Credit original creators

---

### Slide 5: Hook Science for TikTok

**5 Hook Types:**
1. **Visual Hook** - Shocking image
2. **Question Hook** - "Want to know...?"
3. **Statement Hook** - "Stop doing this"
4. **Number Hook** - "7 ways to..."
5. **Story Hook** - "I almost gave up..."

**Target:** >70% retention at 3 seconds

---

### Slide 6: Posting Strategy

**Frequency:** 2-4x daily
**Peak Times:** 12-3 PM, 7-10 PM
**Hashtags:** 3-5 (mix trending + niche)

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Entertainment
- 30% Educational
- 20% Trends
- 10% Promotional

---

### Slide 7: For You Page Optimization

**Do:**
- Use trending audio
- Create short, punchy content
- Include strong hooks
- Encourage engagement
- Post consistently

**Don't:**
- Use low-quality video
- Ignore caption optimization
- Post long intros
- Forget hashtags
- Post irregularly

---

### Slide 8: Engagement Strategies

**Comment Strategy:**
- Reply to all comments (first hour)
- Use questions in captions
- Create comment prompts
- Respond with video

**Community Building:**
- Collaborate with others
- Join trend challenges
- Use duet/stitch
- Engage with followers

---

### Slide 9: Analytics and Optimization

**Key Metrics:**
- **Views:** Total reach
- **Watch Time:** Total minutes watched
- **Completion Rate:** % watched to end
- **Shares:** Viral indicator
- **Saves:** Value indicator

**Benchmark:**
- 10K+ views = Good content
- 50K+ views = Viral content

### Slide 2: Color Psychology

**Colors evoke emotions:**
- **Red:** Passion, urgency
- **Blue:** Trust, calm
- **Yellow:** Optimism, attention
- **Green:** Nature, health
- **Purple:** Luxury, creativity

**Use strategically!**

---

### Slide 3: Platform Color Strategies

**TikTok:** Bright, saturated colors
**Instagram:** Aesthetic, curated
**YouTube:** High contrast thumbnails
**LinkedIn:** Professional, trustworthy
**Pinterest:** Warm, inviting

---

### Slide 4: Audio Power

**Audio = 60% of viewing experience**

**Elements:**
- **Music** (tempo, genre, mood)
- **Sound Effects** (transitions, emphasis)
- **Voiceover** (energetic, calm, conversational)

**Optimization:**
- Music: -20dB to -10dB
- SFX: -15dB to -5dB
- VO: -6dB to 0dB

---

### Slide 5: The 3-Second Hook

**You have 3 seconds to capture attention**

**Hook Types:**
1. Visual Hook (40-60% retention)
2. Question Hook (50-70% retention)
3. Statement Hook (45-65% retention)
4. Number Hook (55-75% retention)
5. Story Hook (60-80% retention)

**Target:** >70% retention

---

### Slide 6: Emotional Targeting

**Primary Emotions:**
- **Joy** (85% engagement)
- **Surprise** (78% engagement)
- **Anger** (72% engagement) - use carefully
- **Fear** (68% engagement) - ethical use only

**Secondary Emotions:**
- Anticipation (65%)
- Trust (60%)
- Love (58%)

---

### Slide 7: Emotional Sequencing

**The Arc:**
1. **Hook (0-3s):** Surprise/curiosity
2. **Build (3-15s):** Empathy/anticipation
3. **Climax (15-30s):** Joy/surprise
4. **Resolution (30-60s):** Satisfaction
5. **CTA (60s+):** Optimism

---

### Slide 8: A/B Testing Psychology

**Test One Variable at a Time:**
- Color (primary color)
- Audio (music tempo)
- Hook (hook type)
- Emotion (emotional trigger)

**Sample Size:** 1,000+ impressions
**Duration:** 24-48 hours
**Confidence:** 95%

---

### Slide 9: Ethical Considerations

**Fear-Based Content:**
- ✅ Offer solution
- ✅ Don't exaggerate
- ✅ Provide value
- ❌ Manipulation
- ❌ False urgency
- ❌ Anxiety marketing
