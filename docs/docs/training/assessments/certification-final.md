# Swarm Expert Certification Assessment

## Overview

This comprehensive assessment tests your knowledge across all training modules. Passing this certification demonstrates your mastery of Swarm Agency operations and qualifies you as a Swarm Expert.

## Assessment Structure

### Part 1: Knowledge (50 points)
- Multiple choice (40 questions, 1 point each)
- True/False (10 questions, 1 point each)

### Part 2: Application (30 points)
- Scenario analysis (5 scenarios, 6 points each)

### Part 3: Practical (20 points)
- Case study analysis (1 case study, 20 points)

**Total:** 100 points
**Passing Score:** 90/100 (90%)
**Time Limit:** 3 hours
**Attempts:** 3 maximum

## Part 2: Application (30 points)

### Scenario 1: Platform Strategy (6 points)

A client wants to grow their fashion brand on social media. They currently have 10K followers on Instagram and are just starting on TikTok. Their target audience is women 18-34.

**Questions:**
1. Which platform should be their primary focus and why? (2 points)
2. What posting strategy would you recommend? (2 points)
3. How would you atomize content across platforms? (2 points)

### Scenario 2: AI Agent Usage (6 points)

You're managing a tech company's LinkedIn presence. The Performance Predictor gives a score of 65 to your latest thought leadership post.

**Questions:**
1. What does this score mean? (1 point)
2. What optimization steps would you take? (3 points)
3. When would you post it? (2 points)

### Scenario 3: Psychology Optimization (6 points)

A fitness brand wants to increase engagement on their YouTube channel. Current videos have low retention and completion rates.

**Questions:**
1. What psychological elements would you test? (2 points)
2. How would you improve their hooks? (2 points)
3. What audio strategies would you recommend? (2 points)

### Scenario 4: Crisis Management (6 points)

A client's post is receiving negative comments about a product issue. Comments are increasing at 50+ per hour, and sentiment is 60% negative.

**Questions:**
1. What crisis level is this? (1 point)
2. What immediate actions would you take? (3 points)
3. How would you communicate with the client? (2 points)

### Scenario 5: Process Excellence (6 points)

You're training a new team member on content atomization. They're struggling to produce quality content efficiently.

**Questions:**
1. What is the bottleneck likely to be? (2 points)
2. How would you structure their workflow? (2 points)
3. What QA checkpoints would you implement? (2 points)

## Part 3: Practical - Case Study (20 points)

### Case Study: EcoStyle Brand Launch

**Background:**
EcoStyle is a new sustainable fashion brand launching their social media presence. They have:
- $5K monthly budget
- Small team (1 founder, 1 part-time content creator)
- No existing social presence
- Mission: Sustainable, affordable fashion
- Target: Women 25-40, eco-conscious, mid-income

**Goals:**
- 10K followers in 3 months
- 5% engagement rate
- 100 sales per month

**Assets:**
- Product photoshoot (20 outfits)
- Founder story video (5 minutes)
- Sustainability documentary (10 minutes)
- Educational content about materials

**Your Task:**

Create a comprehensive 90-day strategy addressing:

1. **Platform Strategy** (5 points)
   - Which platforms to focus on
   - Posting frequency and schedule
   - Content mix by platform

2. **Content Strategy** (5 points)
   - Content atomization plan from existing assets
   - New content needed
   - Psychological optimization approach

3. **AI Agent Implementation** (5 points)
   - How to use each agent
   - Integration into workflow
   - Success metrics

4. **Measurement and Optimization** (5 points)
   - KPIs to track
   - Testing framework
   - Reporting cadence
