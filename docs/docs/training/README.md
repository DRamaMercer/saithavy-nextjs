# Swarm Agency Training Academy

## Overview

Welcome to the Swarm Agency Training Academy - your comprehensive resource for mastering swarm-based social media automation and agency operations.

## Training Paths

### 1. Team Onboarding (Start Here)
**Duration:** 1 week
**Prerequisites:** None

Complete the Team Onboarding Guide to understand:
- Swarm agency architecture and principles
- Your role and responsibilities
- Tool usage and workflows
- Agency standards and best practices

**Start:** `team-onboarding/README.md`

---

### 2. Platform Mastery
**Duration:** 2-4 weeks per platform
**Prerequisites:** Team Onboarding Complete

Master each social media platform:
- TikTok - Short-form video dominance
- Instagram - Visual storytelling and reels
- YouTube - Long-form content and SEO
- Twitter/X - Real-time engagement
- LinkedIn - B2B thought leadership
- Pinterest - Visual search optimization
- Facebook - Community building

**Start:** `platforms/README.md`

---

### 3. AI Agent Certification
**Duration:** 1 week
**Prerequisites:** Team Onboarding Complete

Learn to leverage AI agents:
- Caption Generator - Automated copywriting
- Engagement Router - Smart comment management
- Trend Detector - Viral content identification
- Performance Predictor - Content forecasting

**Start:** `ai-agents/README.md`

---

### 4. Psychology Optimization
**Duration:** 2 weeks
**Prerequisites:** Platform Mastery (1+ platforms)

Master psychological triggers:
- Color psychology for branding
- Audio strategies for retention
- Video hook science
- Emotional targeting methods

**Start:** `psychology/README.md`

---

### 5. Process Excellence
**Duration:** 1 week
**Prerequisites:** AI Agent Certified

Standardize operations:
- Content atomization workflows
- Quality assurance procedures
- Client communication protocols
- Crisis management guidelines

**Start:** `processes/README.md`

## Certification Levels

### Level 1: Swarm Associate
- Complete Team Onboarding

### Level 2: Platform Specialist
- Complete 1+ platform mastery courses
- Pass platform-specific assessments
- Deliver 10+ successful campaigns

### Level 3: AI Operator
- Complete AI Agent certification
- Pass operational assessment
- Achieve 95%+ prediction accuracy

### Level 4: Psychology Master
- Complete psychology optimization
- Pass behavioral analysis quiz
- Demonstrate conversion lift

### Level 5: Swarm Expert
- Complete all training paths
- Pass final certification
- Lead team initiatives
