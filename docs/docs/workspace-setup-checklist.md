# Workspace Setup Checklist
## **Quick-Reference Scannable Version**

*Print this. Post it near your workspace. Check it weekly.*

---

## **🔴 CRITICAL ITEMS (Fix Immediately)**

### **Physical Setup**
- [ ] Monitor at eye level (top of screen at or below eye level)
- [ ] Monitor at arm's length distance (20-26 inches)
- [ ] Desk height allows elbows at 90° when typing
- [ ] Chair supports lumbar (fills gap behind lower back)
- [ ] Feet flat on floor or on footrest
- [ ] Shoulders relaxed (not hunched up)
- [ ] Wrists neutral (not bent up/down)
- [ ] Monitor directly in front (no neck twisting)
- [ ] Keyboard and mouse centered (no reaching)
- [ ] Movement breaks every 30-60 minutes (set timer)

### **Technology**
- [ ] External monitor (24" minimum)
- [ ] External keyboard and mouse
- [ ] Reliable internet (backup hotspot available)
- [ ] Automatic cloud backup enabled
- [ ] Task management system in place
- [ ] Time blocking on calendar
- [ ] Communication boundaries established
- [ ] Start-of-workday ritual
- [ ] End-of-workday ritual
- [ ] Water at desk

## **🚨 RED FLAGS (Seek Professional Help)**

- [ ] Back/neck pain that persists into evening or weekend
- [ ] Headaches that affect vision or cause nausea
- [ ] Numbness or tingling in hands/arms
- [ ] Persistent sadness or loss of motivation
- [ ] Sleep disruption from work stress
- [ ] Eye pain or vision changes

**If you checked any of these:** Make appointment with doctor or physical therapist. Your workspace can't fix medical issues.

## **🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK GUIDE**

| Problem | Immediate Fix | Long-Term Solution |
|---------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Back pain** | Check monitor height, chair lumbar | Standing desk, core strengthening |
| **Eye strain** | Check distance, 20-20-20 rule | Eye exam, computer glasses |
| **Can't focus** | Test different background sounds | Time blocking, communication boundaries |
| **Isolated/lonely** | Schedule virtual coffee | Coworking 1-2x/week, social activities |
| **Work bleeding into life** | End-of-day ritual, hard stop time | Separate device, physical boundary |

## **🎯 SUCCESS CRITERIA**

**You know your workspace is working when:**
- You can sit comfortably for 1+ hours without pain
- You can focus for 90-minute deep work blocks
- You can fully disconnect at end of day
- You wake up excited to work, not dreading discomfort
- You rarely think about your setup (it's just right)

**Truth:** The best workspace is invisible. You stop noticing it and just do great work.

## **📱 REMINDERS (Set These Now)**

**Every 30 minutes:** Stand up and move (timer or app)
**Every 2 hours:** Look out window for 2 minutes
**Midday:** Lunch away from desk (20 minutes minimum)
**End of day:** Shutdown ritual, clear workspace
**Weekly:** 30-minute maintenance review
**Monthly:** Ergonomics reassessment

---

## **🆘 EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS**

**Internet fails:**
- Phone hotspot immediately
- Know local coffee shop with WiFi
- Offline work available (documents downloaded)

**Power outage:**
- Laptop fully charged daily
- UPS battery backup for critical equipment
- Phone hotspot for internet

**Urgent work during personal time:**
- Clear boundaries with team (true emergencies only)
- After-hours response time communicated
- On-call rotation if applicable

**Equipment fails:**
- Backup headset (even cheap earbuds)
- Phone as backup camera
- Local library or coworking space
