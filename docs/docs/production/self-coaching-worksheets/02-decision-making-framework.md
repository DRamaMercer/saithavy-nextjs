### PURPOSE
This framework helps you make high-stakes decisions with clarity, values alignment, and confidence. It transforms agonizing "what should I do?" spirals into structured thinking you can trust.

**Time to complete:** 25-35 minutes
**Best used when:** Facing a career change, business pivot, relationship decision, or any choice that keeps you up at night

### PERSONAL NOTE FROM THE CREATOR

The hardest decision I ever made was leaving a stable career path to start my own business. I spent months agonizing over it, running pros/cons lists until 3am, seeking advice from anyone who would listen.

Here's what nobody told me: **The anxiety doesn't vanish after you decide.**

I thought if I made the "right" choice, I'd feel instant peace. Instead, I felt terrified - but also alive. That's when I learned: Clarity doesn't remove fear. It just gives you a direction to move through it.

Every major decision since then has followed this pattern:
1. Agonize over making the wrong choice
2. Decide with imperfect information
3. Feel terrified (this is normal)
4. Take one small action
5. Adjust course as I learn

This framework captures that process. It won't eliminate the weight of your decision - but it will help you make it with intention, not panic.

**Remember:** You can't know the future. You can only make the best choice with what you know right now, then trust yourself to navigate whatever comes next.

### EMERGENCY DECISION PROTOCOL

*When you need to decide NOW:*

**The 10-10-10 Rule:**
- How will I feel about this in 10 minutes?
- How will I feel about this in 10 months?
- How will I feel about this in 10 years?

**If still stuck, flip a coin.**
Not because it decides for you - but because the moment it's in the air, you'll know what you're HOPING for. That's your answer.
