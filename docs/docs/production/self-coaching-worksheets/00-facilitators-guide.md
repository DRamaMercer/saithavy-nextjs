## OVERVIEW

This Self-Coaching Worksheet Series is designed to help individuals navigate common challenges and transitions with clarity, intention, and self-compassion. Each worksheet combines reflective questions, practical frameworks, and action planning to create transformation through self-guided coaching.

**The Series Includes:**
1. **Clarity Through Chaos** - For feeling stuck or overwhelmed
2. **Decision-Making Framework** - For major life/business decisions
3. **Values Alignment Check** - For living in alignment with core values
4. **Energy Audit** - For identifying what drains/energizes you
5. **Courageous Conversation Prep** - For difficult conversations

**Target Audience:**
- Entrepreneurs and business owners facing decisions and transitions
- Leaders seeking greater self-awareness and effectiveness
- Anyone in a period of growth, change, or uncertainty
- People who want practical tools for personal development

**Core Philosophy:**
The worksheets are built on the **Rebuild Formula**:
- **Truth** - Name what's real without judgment
- **Reframe** - Shift perspective to find opportunity
- **Practice** - Take imperfect action
- **Anchor** - Lock in learning and create sustainable change

## BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES

### Tone Characteristics

**Empathetic + Vulnerable:**
- Share personal struggles, not just successes
- Use "I" statements to create connection
- Acknowledge difficulty without minimizing it
- Validate that challenges are normal

**Signature Phrases:**
- "You're allowed to start small. Just don't stay unclear."
- "Every season is training."
- "The conversation you're avoiding is usually the one you need most."
- "Clarity doesn't remove fear. It just gives you a direction to move through it."

**Tone Slider: Soft → Direct**
- Start with emotional validation (soft)
- Move to practical action (direct)
- End with encouragement (soft)

**Examples of Tone in Practice:**

*Too clinical:*
"Complete the following assessment to identify your core values."

*Brand-aligned:*
"I spent years chasing 'success' before realizing I was living someone else's values. Let's discover what actually matters to YOU - not what you think should matter."

*Too directive:*
"You must set this boundary immediately."

*Brand-aligned:*
"You're allowed to have needs. You're allowed to set boundaries. You're allowed to speak your truth. Here's how to do it in a way that honors both you and the relationship."

## HOW TO USE THIS SERIES

### For Individuals (Self-Guided)

**Getting Started:**
1. Choose the worksheet that matches your current challenge
2. Set aside 20-40 minutes of uninterrupted time
3. Work through each section sequentially
4. Be honest - no one will see this but you
5. Take at least one action step, no matter how small

**Recommended Flow:**
- **Feeling stuck?** Start with Worksheet 1 (Clarity Through Chaos)
- **Facing a big decision?** Use Worksheet 2 (Decision-Making Framework)
- **Feeling "off track"?** Try Worksheet 3 (Values Alignment Check)
- **Constantly drained?** Complete Worksheet 4 (Energy Audit)
- **Avoiding a hard conversation?** Prepare with Worksheet 5 (Courageous Conversation)

**Best Practices:**
- **Don't rush** - The value comes from thoughtful reflection, not quick completion
- **Write freely** - Let your thoughts flow without editing
- **Be specific** - Vague answers lead to vague results
- **Revisit regularly** - These worksheets grow with you; complete quarterly or as needed
- **Share selectively** - Choose trusted people who will honor your vulnerability

---

### For Coaches, Therapists, and Facilitators

**Using Worksheets in Sessions:**

**Before the Session:**
- Review the worksheet in advance
- Choose sections most relevant to your client's current situation
- Prepare to model vulnerability with your own examples

**During the Session:**
1. **Set context** (3 minutes): Explain why this worksheet, why now
2. **Co-create safety** (2 minutes): "This is a judgment-free space. Be as honest as you can."
3. **Work together** (15-20 minutes): Guide client through key sections
4. **Identify insights** (5 minutes): "What's emerging for you?"
5. **Commit to action** (5 minutes): Choose ONE specific next step
6. **Close with acknowledgment** (2 minutes): Validate their courage

**After the Session:**
- Email the full worksheet for deeper reflection
- Schedule follow-up to review progress
- Invite them to bring insights to next session

**Pro Tips for Facilitators:**
- **Model vulnerability** - Share your own struggles with the topic
- **Normalize resistance** - "It's normal to feel stuck here. What's coming up?"
- **Celebrate small wins** - Every insight is progress
- **Avoid "fixing"** - Your role is to guide reflection, not solve problems
- **Trust the process** - The worksheet works if you let it

### For Teams and Groups

**Workshop Format (90 minutes):**

**Introduction (10 minutes)**
- Welcome and context setting
- Why this worksheet matters for the team
- Brief overview of the process

**Individual Work (30 minutes)**
- Silent reflection time
- Complete key sections (not the full worksheet)
- Focus on personal insights

**Small Group Sharing (25 minutes)**
- Break into groups of 3-4
- Share one insight from the worksheet
- Practice active listening
- No fixing or advice-giving

**Large Group Debrief (20 minutes)**
- Volunteer shares (optional)
- Common themes
- Commit to one action
- Close with appreciation

**Virtual Workshop Adaptations:**
- Use breakout rooms for small groups
- Provide worksheet digitally in advance
- Use chat for quieter participants to share
- Record key insights on shared screen

**Team-Specific Applications:**
- **Values Alignment Check** → Build team culture
- **Energy Audit** → Improve meeting structures and work rhythms
- **Courageous Conversation Prep** → Resolve conflicts and improve communication
- **Decision-Making Framework** → Align on key choices

## COMMON QUESTIONS

### For Users

**Q: How often should I complete these worksheets?**
A: Complete each worksheet when you're facing that specific challenge. For ongoing growth, revisit the full series quarterly.

**Q: Can I share these with friends/team?**
A: Yes! These worksheets are most powerful when shared with trusted people who will hold space for your growth.

**Q: What if I get stuck on a question?**
A: Skip it and come back. Sometimes your subconscious needs time to process. Or, try answering "what I want to say is..." and see what emerges.

**Q: Do I need to complete them in order?**
A: No. Each worksheet stands alone. Start with whatever challenge you're facing right now.

**Q: What if my answers change over time?**
A: They will! That's growth, not inconsistency. Revisit worksheets periodically to track your evolution.
