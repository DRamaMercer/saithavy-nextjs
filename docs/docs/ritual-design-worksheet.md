## Quick Ritual Templates

*(Need inspiration? Pick one and customize.)*

### Daily: Morning Check-In
- Time: 9:00 AM
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Format: "How are you showing up? What's on your plate? What support do you need?"

### Daily: Shutdown Ritual
- Time: End of day
- Duration: 5 minutes
- Format: "What did I accomplish? What's on deck for tomorrow? I'm signing off."

### Weekly: Friday Retro
- Time: Friday 4:00 PM
- Duration: 60 minutes
- Format: "What should we start/stop/continue?"

### Weekly: Appreciations Circle
- Time: Friday 4:30 PM
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Format: "I appreciate [teammate] for [specific action]."

### Monthly: Culture Audit
- Time: Last Friday
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Format: "On a scale of 1-10, how's our culture? What needs to change?"

**Less is more, but consistency is everything.**
