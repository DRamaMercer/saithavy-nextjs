## Recording Guidelines

### Voice Performance
- Speed: Slightly slower than conversation
- Tone: Warm, authentic, supportive
- Energy: Calm but not robotic
- Variation: Teaching (normal) → Practice (slower)

### Technical Specs
- Format: WAV 44.1kHz/24-bit (production)
- Distribution: MP3 192-320kbps
- Microphone: Warm, intimate sound
- Processing: Light compression, gentle EQ

### Music Moods by Part
- Part 1: Soft ambient, gentle
- Part 2: Rhythmic but calming
- Part 3: Deep, resonant, serious
- Part 4: Light, hopeful, energizing
