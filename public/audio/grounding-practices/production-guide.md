## Recording Specifications

### Voice Performance
- **Speed:** Slightly slower than normal conversation
- **Tone:** Calm but authentic - not robotic or overly meditative
- **Warmth:** Speak like talking to a close friend
- **Variation:** More energy in teaching, slower in practices
- **Consistency:** Maintain same voice across all 4 parts

### Technical Requirements
- **Microphone:** Warm, intimate sound (close but not too close)
- **Environment:** Quiet space with minimal reverb
- **Format:** High-quality WAV (44.1kHz/24-bit minimum)
- **Processing:** Light compression, gentle EQ, de-essing

### Pacing Guidelines
- **Teaching sections:** Normal conversation pace
- **Practice sections:** 20-30% slower, more spacious
- **Pauses:** Use marked pause times as minimums
- **Breath room:** Leave space after important points
