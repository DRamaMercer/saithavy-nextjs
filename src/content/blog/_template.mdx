### Content Best Practices

1. **Heading Structure**: Always start with H2 (##), never H1 (#)
2. **Excerpt Length**: Keep under 200 characters for optimal SEO
3. **SEO Title**: Under 60 characters for best SERP display

5. **Image Alt Text**: Be descriptive and specific for accessibility
6. **Internal Links**: Use relative paths (/blog/post-slug)
7. **External Links**: Add context, use descriptive anchor text

## SEO Best Practices Checklist

When creating your blog post, ensure:

- [ ] Title is compelling and includes primary keyword
- [ ] Excerpt is under 160 characters and engaging
- [ ] Featured image is optimized (compressed, proper size)
- [ ] H1 used only once (the main title)
- [ ] H2 and H3 headings create clear structure
- [ ] Primary keyword appears in first 100 words
- [ ] Keywords used naturally throughout (avoid stuffing)
- [ ] Internal links to other relevant posts
- [ ] External links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Content is 1000+ words (for better SEO)
- [ ] Paragraphs are short (2-4 sentences)
- [ ] Bullet points and lists for scannability
- [ ] Call-to-action at the end
- [ ] Meta description is compelling
- [ ] Categories and tags are relevant

## Content Guidelines

- **Length**: Aim for 1500-2500 words for in-depth posts
- **Tone**: Conversational yet professional
- **Voice**: First person for personal stories, second person for advice
- **Formatting**: Use bold, italics, and lists strategically
- **Links**: 2-5 internal links, 1-3 external authoritative links
- **Images**: 1 featured + 2-4 supporting images
- **Readability**: Write at 8th-grade level for accessibility
- **Value**: Every paragraph should provide value to the reader
