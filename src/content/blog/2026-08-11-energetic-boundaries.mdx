Some days you wake up full of energy. Everything flows. You're productive, positive, present.

Other days you're depleted before you even start. Drained. Empty. Like someone sucked the life out of you.

What's the difference?

**Energy management.**

Not just physical energy—your whole energetic system. The invisible stuff that powers your work, your relationships, your creativity, your joy.

## What Are Energetic Boundaries?

Energetic boundaries are the invisible lines that protect your energy field.

Like physical boundaries (saying no, leaving a room), energetic boundaries protect your inner space. They keep out energy that doesn't serve you and keep in energy that does.

### Types of Energy Drains

**1. People Drains**
- Energy vampires
- Constant complainers
- People who need more than you can give
- Toxic relationships

**2. Place Drains**
- Chaotic environments
- Negative spaces
- High-stress locations
- Crowded areas

**3. Activity Drains**
- Doom scrolling
- Endless news consumption
- Arguments
- Overcommitting

**4. Digital Drains**
- Social media comparison
- Email overwhelm
- Constant notifications

## Sensing Your Energy

Before you can protect your energy, you need to sense it:

### The Energy Check-In
Throughout the day, ask:
- Where is my energy right now? (1-10)
- What raised it?
- What lowered it?
- Who gives me energy?
- Who drains me?

### Body Signals
Your body tells you:
- Tightness = boundary breach
- Fatigue = energy drain
- Expansion = energy gain
- Contraction = protection needed

## Building Energetic Boundaries

### 1. Energy Awareness
- Track your energy daily
- Notice patterns
- Identify drains and fills

### 2. Quick Protection Tools
- Visualize a shield
- Set intention before entering
- Breath-based reset

### 3. Communication
- "I need to conserve my energy today."
- "I'm at capacity."
- "I need some space."

### 4. Environment Design
- Create energy-friendly spaces
- Limit time in drains
- Curate your inputs

### 5. Relationship Boundaries
- Limit time with drains
- Shorter interactions
- Protective schedules

## The Energy Budget

Like a financial budget, budget your energy:

**Income (Energy Gains):**
- Rest
- Exercise
- Creative work
- Connection
- Nature

**Expenses (Energy Drains):**
- Work
- Relationships
- Chores
- Obligations

**Net = Income - Expenses**

If expenses > income, you go into debt. Energy debt = burnout.

## Daily Energy Protection Ritual

Start and end your day with protection:

### Morning:
1. Check energy level
2. Set intention for protection
3. Visualize boundaries
4. Plan energy-conscious choices

### Evening:
1. Review energy transactions
2. Note what worked
3. Release what didn't
4. Reset for tomorrow
