## Content at a Glance

### Pages 1-2: What is Inclusive Automation?
**Core Question**: "Who are we leaving behind?"
- Why automation excludes (single-path, hidden dependencies, rigid timing)
- How to include everyone (accessibility as innovation, not compliance)
- Business case: reach, retention, risk reduction, innovation
- **Signature**: "Innovation isn't about new tools—it's about better questions"

### Pages 3-5: Accessibility Audit
**Total Score: 0-45 points**
- Input Methods (15): keyboard, screen reader, mobile, voice, alternatives
- Output Formats (15): visual clarity, alt formats, cognitive load, language, errors
- Workflow Flexibility (15): time, multiple paths, error recovery, personalization, edge cases

**Score Interpretation**:
- 0-15: Critical exclusion risks 🚨
- 16-30: Moderate barriers ⚠️
- 31-40: Good foundation ✅
- 41-45: Exemplary inclusion 🌟

### Pages 6-8: 7 Inclusive Design Principles
1. **Multiple Ways to Participate** - Not one-size-fits-all
2. **Accessibility Built-In** - Design from inclusion, not retrofit
3. **Test with Real Diverse Users** - Real people, real barriers
4. **Document Inclusive Decisions** - Why we chose this approach
5. **Plan for Edge Cases** - Edge cases are use cases
6. **Provide Alternatives** - No single point of failure
7. **Iterate Based on Feedback** - Continuous improvement

### Pages 9-11: Implementation Framework (12 Weeks)
1. **Discovery** (Weeks 1-2): Stakeholder interviews, audit, personas, metrics
2. **Design** (Weeks 3-4): Workshops, workflow mapping, prototypes, requirements
3. **Develop** (Weeks 5-8): WCAG implementation, keyboard/screen reader testing
4. **Test** (Weeks 9-10): Usability testing with diverse participants
5. **Launch** (Weeks 11-12): Phased rollout, training, monitoring
6. **Iterate** (Ongoing): Monthly reviews, quarterly feedback, annual audits

### Pages 12-13: Case Studies
1. **Inclusive Onboarding** (Tech startup, 150 employees)
   - 68% → 94% completion, 2.8 → 4.8/5 satisfaction, +18% retention

2. **Accessible Document Workflow** (Healthcare, 500 staff)
   - 100% adoption, zero accessibility complaints, legal compliance

3. **Multi-Channel Task Management** (Remote-first, 200 distributed)
   - 62% → 89% engagement, zero exclusion, 24/7 global workflow

### Pages 14-15: Toolkit + Resources
- **Accessibility Audit Checklist**: 20 essential checks

- **User Testing Protocol**: Recruit diverse participants, document barriers
- **Resources**: WCAG guidelines, testing tools, communities
