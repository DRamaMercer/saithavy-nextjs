## SIGNATURE EXERCISES

### The "What If" Flip
Transform fear-based questions into opportunity-based questions

### The Evening Review
10-minute daily reflection practice:
- What stressed me today?
- What worked well?
- What didn't work?
- What did I learn?

### The Truth Inventory
Radical honesty exercise:
- "Right now, I'm struggling with ___"
- "I've been avoiding ___"
- "I don't want to admit that ___"

### The Rebuild Formula
Applied to real difficulties:
1. Truth: "This is where I am"
2. Reframe: "What's the opportunity?"
3. Practice: "What action will I take?"
4. Anchor: "How do I sustain this?"

### The Stress-Recovery Cycle
1. Stress (controlled challenge)
2. Recovery (rest and integration)
3. Adaptation (come back stronger)

## HOW TO USE THIS WORKBOOK

### For Self-Study
1. Read through introduction (Pages 1-2)
2. Complete assessment (Pages 5-7)
3. Identify your growth edge
4. Learn The Rebuild Formula (Pages 8-10)
5. Read case studies for inspiration (Pages 11-12)
6. Start the 30-day challenge (Pages 13-14)
7. Use resources for ongoing support (Page 15)

### For Coaching/Clients
1. Use assessment as baseline measurement
2. Focus on client's lowest-scoring dimension
3. Teach The Rebuild Formula
4. Assign relevant case study
5. Guide through 30-day challenge
6. Check in weekly on progress
7. Reassess after 30 days

### For Workshops/Groups
1. Everyone completes assessment
2. Share patterns (not scores)
3. Teach framework to group
4. Break into small groups for exercises
5. Case study discussions
6. Start 30-day challenge together
7. Weekly check-in meetings
