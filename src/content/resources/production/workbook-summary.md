## Content Highlights

### The Rebuild Formula (Core Framework)
1. **Truth** - Get honest about where you are (radical assessment without judgment)
2. **Reframe** - Change your perspective (see opportunity in difficulty)
3. **Practice** - Take intentional action (consistent, small actions)
4. **Anchor** - Make it sustainable (systems, environment, community)

### Four Principles of Anti-Fragility
1. **Embrace Uncertainty** - Treat uncertainty as information, not threat
2. **Small Stresses Strengthen** - Use hormesis (controlled stress + recovery)
3. **Reflect and Integrate** - Extract wisdom from every experience
4. **Community is Armor** - Build interdependence and mutual support

### Assessment Dimensions
1. Relationship with Uncertainty (tolerance for the unknown)
2. Stress Response (capacity to grow from stress)
3. Learning from Experience (pattern recognition and integration)
4. Community and Support (quality of support network)
5. Adaptability (flexibility in response to change)
