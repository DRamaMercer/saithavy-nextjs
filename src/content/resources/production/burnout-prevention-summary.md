## Resource Overview

A complete burnout prevention and recovery system featuring:

### 1. 30-Item Early Warning Signs Assessment
- Physical signs (6 items)
- Emotional signs (8 items)
- Mental signs (7 items)
- Behavioral signs (5 items)
- Work patterns (4 items)
- Severity indicators (🟢 Green / 🟡 Yellow / 🔴 Red)
- Scoring system with risk levels
- Critical warning signs requiring immediate intervention

### 2. Immediate Action Items by Zone
- **Green Zone** (Low Risk): Maintenance actions
- **Yellow Zone** (Moderate Risk): Quick recovery protocol
- **Red Zone** (Severe Risk): Emergency protocol
- What to do RIGHT NOW when you notice signs
- Step-by-step actions for each severity level

### 3. Prevention Practices
**Daily Habits (5-10 minutes):**
- Morning intention and mindfulness
- Midday reset breaks
- Evening shutdown rituals
- Gratitude practice

**Weekly Rituals (30-60 minutes):**
- Weekly review and planning
- Self-compassion check
- Recovery time scheduling

**Monthly Deep Dives (2-3 hours):**
- Energy audit
- Commitment review
- Financial check
- Relationship inventory
- Career alignment assessment

**Quarterly Life Audits (Half-day):**
- Review goals and learnings
- Set next quarter intentions
- Life domains assessment (8 areas)
- Schedule vacation and recovery time

### 4. Work Environment Audit
- Physical workspace assessment (ergonomics, lighting, air quality)
- Digital environment check (notifications, boundaries, clutter)
- Schedule & boundaries review (meetings, focus time, breaks)
- Workload analysis (capacity, role clarity, support, recognition)

### 5. Team Culture Assessment
- 20 signs of toxic team culture
- Psychological safety checklist (8 items)
- Support systems inventory (work and personal)
- When to consider leaving your job

### 6. Recovery Protocol (3 Phases)
**Phase 1: Stabilization (Weeks 1-2)**
- Medical foundation (doctor, mental health professional)
- Complete rest (time off, sleep, reduced inputs)
- Basic functioning (food, hydration, gentle movement, nature)
- Emotional support (honest communication, release pressure)

**Phase 2: Recovery (Weeks 3-8)**
- Gradual activity following energy levels
- Daily structure and therapy/coaching
- Root cause analysis
- Rebuilding identity and setting new boundaries
- Light return to activity

**Phase 3: Rebuild (Months 3-6)**
- Sustainable work return
- Ongoing support (therapy, accountability)
- Lifestyle changes (exercise, sleep, nutrition)
- Prevention systems (weekly rituals, boundaries, check-ins)

**Real Recovery Stories:**
- Maria (Software Engineer): 8-month recovery, job change
- James (Marketing Manager): 6-month recovery, quit toxic job
- Priya (Teacher): 10-month recovery, reduced hours

### 7. 30-Day Prevention Challenge
**Week 1: Awareness**
- Daily energy checks
- End-of-day reflection
- Body scan practice
- Take burnout assessment

**Week 2: Boundaries**
- Set one boundary daily
- Schedule recovery time
- End workday ritual
- Boundary audit

**Week 3: Recovery**
- 7+ hours of sleep
- Movement breaks
- Nourish body
- Schedule one recovery day

**Week 4: Integration**
- Morning intention
- Midday reset
- Evening shutdown
- Monthly review
