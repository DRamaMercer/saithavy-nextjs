## 🎙️ Audio Companion Script

**Structure:**

**Introduction (2 minutes)**
- ☑ Welcome + gratitude
- ☑ Vulnerable storytelling (my worst leadership moments)
- ☑ The leadership paradox
- ☑ What is a strategic pause?
- ☑ The 3-question framework preview

**The 3-Question Framework (3 minutes)**
- ☑ Question 1: What Do I Know? (Facts vs. Stories)
  - Story-making machines
  - Example: "They don't respect me" vs. facts
- ☑ Question 2: What Do I Feel? (Emotions as Data)
  - Unfelt emotions drive behavior
  - Example: shame as data
- ☑ Question 3: What's Aligned Action? (Wisdom-Based Decisions)
  - Freeze or react → middle path
  - Example: care + accountability

**Guided Practice (5 minutes)**
- ☑ Get comfortable + 3 deep breaths
- ☑ Think of a stressful situation
- ☑ Practice Question 1: What do I know? (15 sec pause)
- ☑ Practice Question 2: What do I feel? (body scan + emotion inquiry, 15 sec pause)
- ☑ Practice Question 3: What's aligned action? (wisest self, 20 sec pause)
- ☑ Closing breaths (clarity, wisdom, peace)

**Closing**
- ☑ Encouragement (it gets easier)
- ☑ 21-day invitation
- ☑ Systems before willpower
- ☑ Signature lines
- ☑ Thank you + gratitude

**Technical Specs:**
- ☑ Duration: 10:00 minutes
- ☑ Voice speed: 2/3 normal pace
- ☑ Tone: Warm, conversational, vulnerable
- ☑ Music cues: Intro (grounding), Framework (energized), Practice (contemplative), Closing (uplifting)
- ☑ Pauses: Clearly marked with timing

**Word Count:** ~1,500 words
