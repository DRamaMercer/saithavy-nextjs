# AI Innovation Playbook - Quick Start Guide

## Get Started in 15 Minutes

This guide will help you take immediate action on your AI implementation journey.

---

## Step 1: Complete the AI Readiness Assessment (10 minutes)

**Go to**: `01-ai-readiness-assessment.md`

**What You'll Do**:
- Answer 10 questions about your organization
- Score your current AI readiness (10-50 points)
- Identify your strengths and gaps

**What You'll Get**:
- Overall readiness score
- Category breakdowns (Foundation, Leadership, Adoption, Strategy)
- Personalized next steps based on your score

**Quick Reference**:
- **10-20 points**: Beginner - Focus on foundation building
- **21-35 points**: Intermediate - Ready for implementation
- **36-50 points**: Advanced - Ready for rapid deployment

## Step 3: Access Prompt Templates (Immediate)

**Go to**: `04-prompt-library.md`

**Copy-Paste These 3 Prompts to Start**:

### Prompt 1: Content Generation (Marketing)
```
You are a content marketing expert. Write a [CONTENT TYPE] about [TOPIC].

Target audience: [WHO]
Tone: [PROFESSIONAL/FRIENDLY/AUTHORITATIVE]

Include:
- Compelling headline
- Engaging introduction
- 3-5 key sections with subheadings
- Practical examples
- Actionable takeaways
- Strong conclusion

Length: [WORD COUNT]
```

### Prompt 2: Email Response (Customer Service)
```
You are a customer service representative known for helpfulness and empathy.

Draft a response to this customer inquiry:
[PASTE CUSTOMER MESSAGE]

Requirements:
- Acknowledge their concern
- Provide a solution or next steps
- Maintain friendly, professional tone
- End with offer of additional help
- Keep under 200 words
```

### Prompt 3: Analysis (Any Role)
```
You are an expert analyst with strong business acumen.

Analyze this [DATA/CONTENT/SITUATION]:
[PASTE INFORMATION]

Provide:
- Executive summary (3 bullets)
- Key findings (5-7 points)
- Actionable recommendations (3-5 items)
- Potential risks or concerns
- Success metrics to track

Format as a clear, scannable report.
```

## Step 4: Set Up Your AI Tools (15 minutes)

**Recommended Starting Tools**:

### For Text Generation:
- **ChatGPT** (chat.openai.com) - Free or $20/month
- **Claude** (claude.ai) - Free or $20/month
- Both work great for beginners

### Setup Process:
1. Create account (use work email if possible)
2. Explore the interface (5 minutes)
3. Try a simple prompt: "Write a 100-word introduction about AI for business"
4. Review and iterate: "Make it more engaging and include a statistic"
5. Save your best prompts in a document

## Quick ROI Calculation

**Simple Formula**:
```
Monthly Savings = (Hours Saved per Week × 4.3 × Your Hourly Rate)
ROI % = (Annual Savings / Annual Tool Cost) × 100
```

**Example**:
- Tool cost: $20/month = $240/year
- Time saved: 5 hours/week = 21.5 hours/month
- Your rate: $50/hour
- Monthly savings: 21.5 × $50 = $1,075
- Annual savings: $1,075 × 12 = $12,900
- ROI: ($12,900 / $240) × 100 = 5,375%

## When to Get Help

**Self-Service** (You're good if):
- Completed assessment with score 21+
- Clear on use cases and priorities
- Comfortable experimenting independently
- Have 2-3 hours/week to dedicate

**Need Full Support** (Consider if):

- Expand to 2-3 more use cases
- Train broader team
- Integrate into standard workflows
- Plan advanced implementations

**If ROI is marginal**:
- Optimize existing prompts and processes
- Provide additional training

- Consider different use cases

- Review implementation approach
- Check if right use cases selected
- Ensure adequate training
- Don't be afraid to pivot
