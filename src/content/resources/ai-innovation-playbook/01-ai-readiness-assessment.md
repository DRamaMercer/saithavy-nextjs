# AI Readiness Assessment

## Evaluate Your Organization's AI Preparedness

Complete this 10-question assessment to understand your organization's current AI readiness and identify priority areas for improvement.

## Instructions

- Answer each question honestly based on your current state
- Score each question: 1 (Not at all), 2 (Somewhat), 3 (Moderately), 4 (Well), 5 (Extensively)
- Calculate your total score at the end
- Review your readiness level and recommendations

---

## Interpret Your Results

### 10-20 Points: **Beginner Level** - Foundation Building Needed

**Your Situation:**
- Early stages of AI consideration
- Significant gaps in infrastructure, knowledge, and readiness
- High risk of unsuccessful implementation without preparation

**Priority Actions:**
1. Educate leadership on AI potential and risks
2. Assess and organize data infrastructure
3. Build foundational technical capabilities
4. Start with small, low-risk experiments
5. Invest in team training and awareness

**Recommended Starting Point:** Begin with education and awareness before attempting implementation.

---

### 21-35 Points: **Intermediate Level** - Ready for Strategic Implementation

**Your Situation:**
- Some foundational elements in place
- Mixed readiness across different areas
- Good candidate for phased implementation

**Priority Actions:**
1. Address identified gaps (typically data or change management)
2. Select 2-3 high-impact, low-complexity use cases
3. Build cross-functional AI implementation team
4. Develop detailed implementation roadmap
5. Establish governance and measurement frameworks

**Recommended Starting Point:** Ready to begin 90-day implementation plan with focused pilot projects.

---

### 36-50 Points: **Advanced Level** - Ready for Rapid Deployment

**Your Situation:**
- Strong foundation across most areas
- Leadership commitment and resources available
- Positioned for accelerated implementation

**Priority Actions:**
1. Launch multiple concurrent initiatives
2. Develop comprehensive AI transformation roadmap
3. Build Center of Excellence for AI capabilities
4. Implement advanced use cases with high ROI potential
5. Scale successful implementations across organization

**Recommended Starting Point:** Ready for aggressive 90-day timeline with multiple workstreams.

## Next Steps Based on Your Assessment

### Regardless of Your Score:

1. **Document Your Baseline**: Save your assessment results to measure progress
2. **Identify Quick Wins**: Look for low-complexity, high-impact opportunities
3. **Build Your Team**: Identify champions across departments
4. **Set Clear Goals**: Define what success looks like in 90 days
5. **Start Learning**: Begin team education and awareness programs

### If You Scored 10-20:
- Focus on **Phase 1 (Foundation)** for the full 90 days
- Prioritize education and infrastructure
- Consider bringing in AI consultants/advisors
- Set realistic expectations for timeline

### If You Scored 21-35:
- Ready for **standard 90-day implementation**
- Balance quick wins with foundational improvements
- Build internal capabilities while executing
- Measure and communicate early successes

### If You Scored 36-50:
- Ready for **accelerated 60-90 day timeline**
- Launch multiple simultaneous initiatives
- Focus on scaling and optimization
- Develop long-term AI transformation strategy
