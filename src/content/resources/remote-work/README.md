# Remote Work Mastery Guide

**The definitive guide for sustainable, productive remote work**

---

## Overview

This guide is the result of 5+ years of remote work experience across 4 different companies, plus insights from helping dozens of teams transition to remote work.

**The core insight:** Remote work isn't about location. It's about systems.

The people who thrive remotely don't have better discipline or fancier equipment. They have better systems for:
- Setting boundaries that actually stick
- Communicating without constant meetings
- Staying productive without burning out
- Building trust with teams they never see

This guide gives you those systems.

### 🚧 Module 6: Work-Life Boundary Tools
**File:** `06-work-life-boundary-tools.md`

The #1 challenge of remote work: Your work is always there.

**Three types of boundaries (all three essential):**
1. **Physical boundaries:** Separate spaces (even if transformed)
2. **Temporal boundaries:** Separate times (hard stop, shutdown ritual)
3. **Mental boundaries:** Separate mindsets (identity shift, decompression)

**Includes:**
- Fake commute (10-20 minute transition ritual)
- Work shutdown ritual (15 minutes for closure)
- Time blocking (protect focus time)
- Calendar hygiene (manage your schedule)
- Technology boundaries (remove work apps from phone)
- Communicating boundaries (to team, family, friends)
- Troubleshooting boundary failures

**Result:** Stop working at 11pm. Be present for your life. Sustainable remote work.
