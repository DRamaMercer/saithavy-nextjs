## The Priority Decision Framework

### 5 Assessment Questions

Answer these 5 questions honestly (1-5 scale):

**Q1: What's your biggest current challenge?**
- 1 = No real challenges, everything's fine
- 3 = Some challenges but manageable
- 5 = Overwhelmed, barely keeping up

**Q2: How much time can you dedicate weekly?**
- 1 = Less than 2 hours
- 3 = 2-5 hours
- 5 = 5+ hours

**Q3: Are you leading others (team, family, projects)?**
- 1 = No, solo focus only
- 3 = Sometimes, informal leadership
- 5 = Yes, formal leadership role

**Q4: What's your urgency level?**
- 1 = No urgency, want to grow gradually
- 3 = Moderate urgency, want results in 3-6 months
- 5 = High urgency, need results now

**Q5: What systems do you already use?**
- 1 = None, starting from scratch
- 3 = 1-2 productivity systems in place
- 5 = Multiple systems, want to optimize

### Part-Time vs Full-Time Implementation

**Part-Time (2-5 hours/week available):**
- Choose: New Leader or Overwhelmed roadmaps
- Extend each phase by 1-2 weeks
- Focus on 1 system at a time
- Accept slower progress, but still progress

**Full-Time (5+ hours/week available):**
- Any roadmap works
- Follow timelines as written
- Can implement multiple systems faster
- Accelerated results possible

**Key Principle:**
**Consistency beats intensity.**
**Better to do 30 minutes daily than 4 hours once a week.**

## Success Metrics

### 30-Day Check-in

**You're on track if:**
- [ ] Completed 3+ modules from your starting system
- [ ] Daily practice happening 4+ days/week
- [ ] Feeling some positive shift (even small)
- [ ] Haven't skipped more than 3 days in a row

**Red flags (course correct if:**
- ❌ Haven't started any modules
- ❌ Daily practice rarely happening
- ❌ Feeling more overwhelmed than before
- ❌ Skipped 7+ days in a row

**If red flags appear:**
1. Reassess your time commitment (reduce if needed)
2. Return to fundamentals (simplify your approach)
3. Focus on ONE small win per day
4. Reach out for community support

---

### 60-Day Check-in

**You're on track if:**
- [ ] Completed 6+ modules total
- [ ] Added second system (if roadmap calls for it)
- [ ] Seeing measurable improvements in your focus area
- [ ] Daily practice is habit (automatic, not forced)

**Red flags (course correct if:**
- ❌ Still stuck in first system only
- ❌ Not seeing any results yet
- ❌ Motivation declining
- ❌ Considering quitting

**If red flags appear:**
1. Revisit your "why" (what motivated you to start?)
2. Celebrate small wins you've had
3. Adjust approach (not goals)
4. Connect with others for encouragement
