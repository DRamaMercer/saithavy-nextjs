# ROI Calculator

## Measure and Demonstrate the Business Impact of Your AI Implementation

Use this calculator to estimate ROI, track actual results, and communicate value to stakeholders. Includes formulas, templates, and real-world examples.

---

## Quick ROI Estimate

### The Simple Formula

```
ROI (%) = ((Financial Gain - Investment Cost) / Investment Cost) × 100
```

### Example Calculation

**Scenario**: Implementing AI for content creation

**Investment Costs**:
- AI tool subscription: $100/month × 12 = $1,200
- Training time: 20 hours × $75/hour = $1,500
- Implementation time: 40 hours × $75/hour = $3,000
- **Total Investment: $5,700**

**Financial Gains** (Year 1):
- Reduced freelancer costs: $3,000/month × 12 = $36,000
- Increased content output (2x): Value = $20,000
- Improved SEO performance: Value = $8,000
- **Total Gains: $64,000**

**ROI Calculation**:
```
ROI = (($64,000 - $5,700) / $5,700) × 100
ROI = ($58,300 / $5,700) × 100
ROI = 1,023%
```

**Payback Period**: $5,700 / ($64,000 ÷ 12) = 1.07 months

## Common ROI Mistakes to Avoid

### Don't:
- Only count direct financial returns (ignore intangible benefits)
- Use best-case scenarios (be realistic)
- Forget ongoing costs (training, maintenance, optimization)
- Measure too early (allow time for adoption and optimization)
- Compare to zero baseline (compare to current alternatives)

### Do:
- Track both leading and lagging indicators
- Measure consistently over time
- Include qualitative benefits
- Benchmark against industry standards
- Communicate regularly with stakeholders
- Adjust calculations based on actual data
