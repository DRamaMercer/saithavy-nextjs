## CONTENT CREATION PROMPTS (20)

### Blog Posts & Articles

**1. Comprehensive Blog Post Generator**
```
You are a content marketing expert. Write a comprehensive blog post about [TOPIC] that includes:

- An attention-grabbing headline
- A compelling introduction that hooks the reader
- 3-5 key sections with subheadings
- Practical examples and data points
- Actionable takeaways
- A strong conclusion with a call-to-action

Target audience: [AUDIENCE]
Tone: [PROFESSIONAL/FRIENDLY/AUTHORITATIVE]
Length: [WORD COUNT]

Include SEO meta title and description.
```

**2. Industry Insight Post**
```
Write an insightful blog post about [TREND/INDUSTRY DEVELOPMENT] that positions [COMPANY] as a thought leader.

Include:
- Current state of the trend
- Why it matters for [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- Expert analysis and predictions
- How [COMPANY] is addressing this
- Practical recommendations
```

**3. How-To Guide**

**5. Listicle Article**

## How to Use These Prompts

### Best Practices

1. **Customize the Brackets**: Replace all bracketed text with your specific information
2. **Provide Context**: Give the AI relevant background, data, or examples
3. **Iterate**: Refine outputs by providing feedback and requesting revisions
4. **Test and Measure**: Track which prompts work best for your use cases
5. **Build Your Library**: Save your best custom prompts for reuse

### Advanced Tips

- **Chain Prompts**: Use outputs from one prompt as input for another
- **Specify Format**: Request specific formats (table, bullet points, sections)
- **Set Constraints**: Define length, style, tone, or structure requirements
- **Provide Examples**: Give examples of desired output style
- **Iterate in Real-Time**: Have a conversation to refine and improve
