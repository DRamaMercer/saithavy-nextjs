## Tips for Success

### 1. Start Small
Don't boil the ocean. Begin with 2-3 quick wins that demonstrate clear value.

### 2. Measure Everything
Establish baseline metrics before starting. Track performance continuously.

### 3. Enable Your Team
Focus on change management and training. Technology is easy; people are hard.

### 4. Iterate and Improve
Your first attempts won't be perfect. Learn, refine, and optimize over time.

### 5. Share Success
Celebrate wins publicly. Social proof drives adoption.

### 6. Stay Focused on Outcomes
AI is a tool, not the goal. Focus on business problems you're solving.
