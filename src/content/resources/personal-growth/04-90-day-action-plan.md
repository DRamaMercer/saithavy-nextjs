# 90-Day Action Plan

## Plan Overview

This is your complete 90-day roadmap. It's designed to take you from where you are NOW to where you want to be through structured, progressive action.

**The Philosophy:**
- Month 1: FOUNDATION - Build the base
- Month 2: MOMENTUM - Accelerate progress
- Month 3: INTEGRATION - Make it permanent

**How to Use This Plan:**
1. Read through the entire plan first
2. Customize it to your specific goals
3. Execute week by week
4. Review and adjust every Sunday
5. Never skip two weeks in a row

---

**What is a Keystone Habit?**
A keystone habit is ONE small habit that triggers positive change across multiple areas of your life.

#### Week 3 Actions

**Reduce Friction for Good Habits:**

For your keystone habit:
- [ ] Prepare everything the night before
- [ ] Put supplies in visible, easy-to-reach location
- [ ] Schedule it at your optimal time
- [ ] Link it to an existing habit
- [ ] Make it SO SMALL it's easy

**Add Friction to Bad Habits:**
- [ ] Remove temptations from environment
- [ ] Add steps before you can do the bad habit
- [ ] Make the alternative more convenient
- [ ] Use "if-then" planning

**Example:**
"If I want to check social media, first I must:
1. Put phone in another room
2. Do 10 pushups
3. Wait 10 minutes
THEN I can check social media"
