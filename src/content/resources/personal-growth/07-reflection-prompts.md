# Reflection Prompts & Journaling Exercises

## The Power of Reflection

**Why Reflection Transforms:**

Reflection is the bridge between experience and wisdom. Without reflection, you repeat patterns. With reflection, you evolve.

**The Science:**
- Reflection increases learning retention by 70%
- Journaling reduces stress by 40%
- Self-reflection boosts emotional intelligence by 35%
- Writing about goals increases achievement by 42%

**How to Use These Prompts:**
- Choose 1-2 prompts per session
- Write without censoring yourself
- Be honest, not harsh
- Focus on insights, not judgments
- Review your responses weekly
