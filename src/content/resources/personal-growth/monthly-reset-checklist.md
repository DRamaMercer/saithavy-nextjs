# Monthly Reset Checklist
## Declutter Commitments and Realign with Core Priorities

---

*A Comprehensive Monthly Practice for Clearing Mental Space, Realigning with Values, and Setting Intententional Direction*

---

## How to Use This Checklist

**Time Required:** 60-90 minutes

**Best Time:** Last Sunday or first Monday of each month

**What You'll Need:**
- This checklist
- Your calendar
- Your goals/vision (if you have them)
- A journal or notes app
- A beverage of your choice

**The Monthly Reset Flow:**
1. **Clear** — Declutter tasks, commitments, and mental space
2. **Review** — Assess the month that passed
3. **Learn** — Extract wisdom and patterns
4. **Realign** — Connect back to your values and vision
5. **Design** — Plan the month ahead
6. **Commit** — Set your intentions and take first action

---
