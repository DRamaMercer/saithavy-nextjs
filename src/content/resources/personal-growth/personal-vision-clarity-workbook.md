## Chapter 2: Uncovering Your Core Values

### What Are Core Values?

Core values are the fundamental beliefs that guide your behavior and decisions. They're your internal compass—what you consider most important in life.

When your actions align with your values, you feel fulfilled and authentic. When they don't, you feel conflicted, drained, or "off."

## Chapter 7: Living Your Vision Daily

### Making Your Vision Real

A vision comes alive through consistent, small actions—not heroic efforts.

---

### The Vision Review Process

**Daily Review (5 minutes):**
- Morning: Set one intention aligned with your vision
- Evening: Reflect on how you honored your vision

**Weekly Review (30 minutes):**
- What progress did I make?
- What did I learn?
- What needs adjustment?
- What are my priorities for next week?

**Monthly Review (1 hour):**
- Am I closer to my vision?
- What's working? What's not?
- What do I need to start, stop, or continue?
- Do my goals still feel aligned?

**Quarterly Review (2 hours):**
- Am I living my values?
- Is my vision still true?
- What needs to change?
- What am I celebrating?

---

### When You Feel Off Track

It's normal to drift. Here's how to realign:

1. **Notice** — Acknowledge you're off track without judgment
2. **Pause** — Take time to reflect on why
3. **Reconnect** — Re-read your vision and values
4. **Choose** — Select ONE action to realign
5. **Forgive** — Let go of guilt and recommit
