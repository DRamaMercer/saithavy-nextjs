# Gap Analysis Worksheet

## Worksheet Overview

The Gap Analysis is your reality check. It identifies the EXACT distance between where you are and where you want to be.

**Why this matters:**
- Most people underestimate their starting point
- Most people overestimate what they can achieve quickly
- This worksheet creates REALISTIC, ACHIEVABLE goals

**Complete this worksheet BEFORE starting your 90-day plan.**

---

## Part 4: Gap Prioritization

**Time Required:** 15 minutes
**Format:** Strategic prioritization

### Instructions

You cannot close ALL gaps in 90 days. Choose strategically.

---

### The 3-Point Prioritization Framework

**For each gap, rate:**

1. **Impact** (1-10): How much would closing this gap improve my life?
2. **Urgency** (1-10): How critical is this to address NOW?
