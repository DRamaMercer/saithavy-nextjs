# Morning Momentum Audio Practice
## Start Each Day with Purpose, Calm, and Momentum

---

*A Guided Morning Practice to Center Yourself, Set Clear Intentions, and Launch Your Day with Focused Energy*

---

## How to Use This Practice

**Duration:** 10-15 minutes

**Best Time:** Immediately upon waking (before checking phone)

**Setting:** Quiet space, seated comfortably

**Equipment Needed:**
- This script (or recorded version)
- Optional: Journal for writing

**Practice Flow:**
1. **Arrive** — Ground into the present moment
2. **Connect** — Check in with yourself
3. **Clarify** — Set your intention
4. **Visualize** — See your day unfold
5. **Activate** — Step into action

---

## The Full Morning Momentum Practice

### Part 1: Arrive (2 Minutes)

*(Script begins with gentle voice)*

**Welcome to this Morning Momentum practice.**

**Find a comfortable seated position. Feet flat on the floor. Hands resting in your lap or on your thighs. Spine tall but relaxed. Shoulders dropping away from your ears.**

**Close your eyes. Or soften your gaze to a spot on the floor in front of you.**

**Let's begin by simply arriving.**

**Take a deep breath in through your nose... filling your lungs completely.**

**And exhale slowly through your mouth... letting everything go.**

*(pause 5 seconds)*

**One more time. Inhale deeply...**

**Exhale fully.**

*(pause 5 seconds)*

**Notice your body. Without changing anything, just notice.**

**Notice where your body makes contact with the chair or floor.**

**Notice any sensations—warmth, coolness, tingling.**

**Notice your breath—the rhythm, the depth, the quality.**

*(pause 10 seconds)*

**You are here. You have arrived.**

**This is a new day. A fresh start. A blank page.**

**Whatever happened yesterday is gone. Whatever tomorrow holds is not here yet.**

**Right here, right now—this moment is yours.**

---

### Part 2: Connect (3 Minutes)

**Now, let's connect with yourself.**

**Gently ask yourself: How am I, right now?**

**Not how should I be. Not how I want to be. How am I actually, right now?**

**Notice what comes up.**

**Physically—what sensations are present?**

**Is there tension anywhere? Energy or fatigue? Lightness or heaviness?**

**No judgment. Just noticing.**

*(pause 10 seconds)*

**Emotionally—what's the emotional weather inside?**

**Calm or stormy? Bright or cloudy? Warm or cool?**

**Whatever you're feeling is welcome here.**

**You don't need to fix it. You don't need to change it.**

**Just let it be felt.**

*(pause 10 seconds)*

**Mentally—what's the quality of your thoughts right now?**

**Racing or still? Clear or foggy? Focused or scattered?**

**Again, just notice. No need to intervene. Just witness.**

*(pause 10 seconds)*

**Take a deep breath.**

**As you exhale, let yourself be exactly as you are.**

**This is your starting point for the day.**

**Honesty with yourself is the foundation of momentum.**

**You begin from where you actually are—not where you think you should be.**

---

### Part 3: Clarify (3 Minutes)

**Now, let's set your intention for the day.**

**Ask yourself: What do I want to create today?**

**Not your to-do list. Not your obligations.**

**What do you want to CREATE?**

**What energy do you want to bring into the world?**

**How do you want to feel?**

**How do you want to show up for yourself?**

**For others?**

**For your work?**

*(pause 15 seconds for reflection)*

**Choose one word that captures your intention for today.**

**Maybe it's:** Presence. Courage. Calm. Focus. Kindness. Energy. Creativity. Love.

**What's your word?**

**Hold it in your mind.**

**Say it silently to yourself.**

*(pause 5 seconds)*

**Now, let's anchor this word in your body.**

**Repeat your word silently with each breath for the next minute.**

**Inhale... [your word].**

**Exhale... [your word].**

**Inhale... [your word].**

**Exhale... [your word].**

*(continue for 1 minute)*

**This word is your North Star for the day.**

**When things get busy or challenging, return to this word.**

**Let it guide your decisions. Your responses. Your energy.**

---

### Part 4: Visualize (3 Minutes)

**Now, let's see your day unfolding.**

**In your mind's eye, picture your day ahead.**

**See yourself moving through the day with your intention guiding you.**

**See yourself handling your first task with focus and presence.**

**See yourself in your first interaction—bringing the energy of your word.**

**See yourself navigating challenges with clarity and grace.**

**See yourself making choices aligned with your values.**

*(pause 10 seconds)*

**Now, identify your ONE key action for today.**

**The one thing that, if you do it, will make today a success.**

**What is it?**

**See yourself doing it.**

**Feel the satisfaction of completion.**

*(pause 10 seconds)*

**Now, acknowledge what might get in the way.**

**What distractions might arise? What fears? What old patterns?**

**See them, but don't let them stop you.**

**See yourself noticing them... and choosing differently.**

**See yourself returning to your intention.**

**See yourself staying true to your word.**

*(pause 10 seconds)*

**This is the day you're creating.**

**Not by hoping. Not by wishing.**

**But by choosing—again and again.**

---

### Part 5: Activate (2 Minutes)

**Now, it's time to activate.**

**Bring your awareness back to your breath.**

**Take three energizing breaths.**

**Inhale sharply through the nose...**

**Exhale forcefully through the mouth.**

**One more. Inhale sharply...**

**Exhale forcefully.**

**One last time. Inhale...**

**Exhale.**

*(pause 5 seconds)*

**Gently wiggle your fingers and toes.**

**Roll your shoulders back.**

**Stretch your arms overhead.**

**When you're ready, slowly open your eyes.**

**Take in your surroundings.**

**You are awake. You are aware. You are ready.**

---

### Closing

**Before you move into your day, make this commitment:**

**"Today, I choose to be [your word]."**

**"Today, I choose to focus on [your one action]."**

**"Today, I choose to show up as the person I'm becoming."**

**This is your day.**

**Live it on purpose.**

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I fall back asleep?**
A: Sit up or stand for the practice. Keep lights dim but not dark.

**Q: What if my mind races the whole time?**
A: That's normal. The practice is noticing the racing, not stopping it. Each time you notice and return to breath, you're strengthening your awareness muscle.

**Q: What if I don't have 15 minutes?**
A: Use the 3-minute version. A short practice done consistently is better than a long practice done occasionally.

**Q: Should I do this before or after coffee?**
A: Before. Your nervous system will respond better without caffeine initially. Have your coffee after as your reward.

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: Begin again. No drama. No guilt. Just begin again.
