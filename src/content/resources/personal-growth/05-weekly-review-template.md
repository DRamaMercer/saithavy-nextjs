# Weekly Review Template

## Why Weekly Reviews Matter

Weekly reviews are the compass that keeps you on track. Without them, you drift. With them, you steer.

**The Science:**
- Regular review increases goal achievement by 33%
- Weekly reflection accelerates learning by 50%
- Accountability boosts completion rates by 65%

**When to Do Your Review:**
- **Best time:** Sunday evening (7-8pm works for most)
- **Duration:** 20-30 minutes
- **Location:** Quiet, distraction-free
- **Tools:** This template + your tracking system

---
