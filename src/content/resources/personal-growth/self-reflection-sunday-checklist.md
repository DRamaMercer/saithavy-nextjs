# Self-Reflection Sunday Checklist
## Review Progress, Learn Quickly, and Reset Priorities

---

*A Weekly Practice for Intentional Living, Faster Learning, and Consistent Progress Toward What Matters*

---

## How to Use This Checklist

**Time Required:** 30-45 minutes

**Best Time:** Sunday afternoon or evening

**What You'll Need:**
- This checklist
- Your calendar/planner
- A journal or notes app
- A quiet space

**The Sunday Ritual Flow:**
1. **Review** — Look back at your week
2. **Learn** — Extract lessons and insights
3. **Celebrate** — Acknowledge wins and progress
4. **Release** — Let go of what didn't serve you
5. **Plan** — Set up the week ahead
