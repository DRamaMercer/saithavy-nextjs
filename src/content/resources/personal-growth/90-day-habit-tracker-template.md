# 90-Day Habit Tracker Template
## Design, Track, and Sustain Identity-Based Habits

---

*A Structured System for Building Habits That Last by Focusing on Identity, Not Just Outcomes*

---

## How to Use This Template

This 90-day habit tracker is designed around the science of habit formation:

1. **Start with identity** — Who do you want to become?
2. **Choose 1-3 habits** — Quality over quantity
3. **Track daily** — What gets measured gets managed
4. **Review weekly** — Learn and adjust
5. **Celebrate progress** — Reinforce positive behavior

**Recommended Protocol:**
- Print or copy this template
- Choose 1-3 habits maximum
- Commit to 90 days
- Don't skip twice in a row

- ❌ = Missed (life happens—don't skip twice!)
