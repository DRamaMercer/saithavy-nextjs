Phase 2: Pilot (Months 3-4)

☐ Implement first use case
☐ Train team members
☐ Collect feedback and iterate
☐ Measure results against baseline

Phase 3: Scale (Months 5-8)

☐ Expand to additional use cases
☐ Integrate AI into more workflows
☐ Optimize based on pilot learnings

Phase 4: Optimize (Months 9-12)

☐ Full deployment of AI strategy
☐ Establish AI governance framework
☐ Plan for next year's initiatives

💡 Tips for Success:
  • Start small with high-impact, low-effort projects
  • Involve your team early in the process
  • Measure everything - you can't improve what you don't measure
  • Plan for iteration - your first attempt won't be perfect
