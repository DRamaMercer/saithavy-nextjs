## Critical Bug Escalation Protocol

### Severity Levels

#### P0 - Critical (Launch Blocker)
- **Definition**: Website down, downloads not working, email not sending
- **Response Time**: Immediate (within 15 minutes)
- **Resolution Time**: Within 2 hours
- **Communication**: Notify all users (if applicable)

#### P1 - High (Urgent)
- **Definition**: Major feature broken, significant UX issue
- **Response Time**: Within 1 hour
- **Resolution Time**: Within 24 hours
- **Communication**: Notify affected users

#### P2 - Medium (Important)
- **Definition**: Minor feature broken, small UX issue
- **Response Time**: Within 4 hours
- **Resolution Time**: Within 1 week
- **Communication**: Log in backlog, fix in next sprint

#### P3 - Low (Nice to Have)
- **Definition**: Cosmetic issue, enhancement request
- **Response Time**: Within 24 hours
- **Resolution Time**: Within 1 month
- **Communication**: Add to roadmap
