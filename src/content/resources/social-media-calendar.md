# Social Media Calendar: 90-Day Content Strategy

## Overview

- **Duration**: 90 days (Q1 2026)
- **Platforms**: LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram
- **Posting Frequency**:
  - LinkedIn: 4-5 posts/week
  - Twitter/X: 10-15 posts/week
  - Instagram: 4-5 posts/week + Stories daily
- **Content Mix**: 40% resources, 30% value-add, 20% engagement, 10% promotional

---

## Platform-Specific Strategies

### LinkedIn Strategy
**Audience**: Professionals, leaders, entrepreneurs, founders
**Content Focus**: Thought leadership, actionable insights, resource downloads
**Best Times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM, 5-6 PM
**Post Format**: Text posts, carousels, PDFs, video clips

**Optimal Length**:
- Text posts: 1,300-1,500 characters (3-minute read)
- Carousels: 8-12 slides
- Video: 30-90 seconds

**Hashtag Strategy** (3-5 per post):
- #MindfulLeadership
- #AIautomation
- #PersonalGrowth
- #RemoteWork
- #LeadershipDevelopment
- #ProductivityTips
- #SmallBusinessTips

---

### Twitter/X Strategy
**Audience**: Tech community, entrepreneurs, productivity enthusiasts
**Content Focus**: Quick tips, threads, resource links, engagement
**Best Times**: Weekdays 8-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
**Post Format**: Text, threads, images, short videos

**Optimal Length**:
- Tweets: Under 280 characters
- Threads: 5-10 tweets
- Focus: Single actionable insight per tweet

**Hashtag Strategy** (1-2 per tweet):
- #MindfulLeadership
- #AI
- #RemoteWork
- #ProductivityHacks

---

### Instagram Strategy
**Audience**: Visual learners, younger professionals, lifestyle-focused
**Content Focus**: Aesthetic graphics, reels, carousel education, behind-the-scenes
**Best Times**: Monday-Friday, 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM
**Post Format**: Feed posts, Reels, Stories, Lives

**Content Types**:
- **Feed**: Educational carousels, quote graphics, resource previews
- **Reels**: Quick tips, transformations, tutorials

## Engagement Strategy

### Community Building Tactics

#### LinkedIn Engagement
- **Comment on** 10 posts daily in your niche
- **Respond to** all comments within 1 hour
- **Ask questions** at the end of posts (boosts comments)
- **Mention** people when relevant (increases reach)
- **Celebrate** others' wins (builds relationships)

#### Twitter/X Engagement
- **Reply to** 20 tweets daily
- **Quote tweet** valuable content with added insight
- **Participate in** relevant Twitter chats (#LeadershipChat, #RemoteWorkChat)
- **Retweet** valuable content 5-10x/day (80/20 rule: 80% others, 20% you)
- **Use threads** to tell stories (higher engagement than single tweets)

#### Instagram Engagement
- **Reply to** all comments with questions
- **Engage with** hashtags (find relevant posts, comment genuinely)
- **Use interactive stickers** in Stories (polls, questions, quizzes)
- **Go Live** weekly for Q&A sessions
- **Collaborate** with complementary accounts (joint Lives, shoutouts)

## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### LinkedIn Best Practices

#### Do's
- [ ] Post natively (no external links in post body)
- [ ] Use link in comments strategy
- [ ] Write conversationally (1st person, storytelling)
- [ ] Format with white space (easy to scan)
- [ ] Include clear CTA
- [ ] Engage before/after posting (15 min before, 1 hour after)
- [ ] Use carousels for education (high engagement)
- [ ] Tag people/companies when relevant

#### Don'ts
- [ ] Don't post links in post body (algorithm penalizes)
- [ ] Don't sell too hard (value first, pitch second)
- [ ] Don't ignore comments (respond within 1 hour)
- [ ] Don't use more than 5 hashtags
- [ ] Don't post more than 1x/day (quality > quantity)

---

### Twitter/X Best Practices

#### Do's
- [ ] Tweet threads (higher engagement)
- [ ] Use images/videos (boosts engagement 2-3x)
- [ ] Tweet consistently (algorithm rewards activity)
- [ ] Engage with big accounts (reply to thought leaders)
- [ ] Use 1-2 hashtags max
- [ ] Keep tweets under 280 characters (threads for longer)
- [ ] Pin your best tweet (resource link)

#### Don'ts
- [ ] Don't link dump (space out links)
- [ ] Don't overuse hashtags (looks spammy)
- [ ] Don't ignore mentions/replies
- [ ] Don't post the same content repeatedly

---

### Instagram Best Practices

#### Do's
- [ ] Use Reels (highest reach currently)
- [ ] Post educational carousels (high saves)
- [ ] Use Stories daily (builds community)
- [ ] Engage with hashtags (find your community)
- [ ] Use 15-20 hashtags (mix of popular + niche)
- [ ] Post consistently (algorithm rewards frequency)
- [ ] Use interactive stickers (boosts engagement)
- [ ] Go Live (biggest reach boost)

#### Don'ts
- [ ] Don't buy followers
- [ ] Don't use banned hashtags
- [ ] Don't over-post (quality > quantity, max 2x/day)
- [ ] Don't ignore DMs (respond within 24 hours)

## Crisis Management & Community Guidelines
