# Weekly Leadership Reflection Template

**A Structured Framework for Tracking Wins, Identifying Blind Spots, and Clarifying Priorities**

---

## Introduction: Why Weekly Reflection?

**Leaders who reflect regularly outperform those who don't.** It's that simple.

Weekly reflection creates:

- **Pattern recognition** — You see themes you miss in the moment
- **Accelerated learning** — You extract lessons from experience
- **Intentional course correction** — You adjust before problems compound
- **Renewed clarity** — You reconnect with what matters most
- **Sustainable growth** — You integrate learning rather than repeating mistakes

This template is designed to take 20-30 minutes once a week. Schedule it—Friday afternoon, Sunday evening, or Monday morning all work. What matters is consistency.

---
