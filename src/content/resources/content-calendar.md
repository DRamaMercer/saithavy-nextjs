## Email Newsletter Integration

### Newsletter Segments
1. **New Subscribers**: Starter kit sequence (5 emails)
2. **Personal Growth Segment**: Goal & habit resources
3. **Leadership Segment**: Management & team resources
4. **Remote Work Segment**: Productivity & workspace resources
5. **AI/Automation Segment**: Tech & workflow resources

### Email Cadence
- **Primary Newsletter**: Weekly (every Wednesday)
  - 1-2 featured resources
  - Blog post highlight
  - User testimonial
  - Upcoming resource tease

- **Segmented Emails**: Bi-weekly (Tuesdays)
  - Category-specific resources
  - Deep-dive content
  - Exclusive offers

- **Launch Emails**: Per resource release
  - Day 0: Launch announcement
  - Day 3: Social proof reminder
  - Day 7: Last chance

### Email Subject Line Formulas
1. **Curiosity**: "The one tool [successful group] uses for [benefit]"
2. **Numbers**: "5 resources to [achieve goal] in [timeframe]"
3. **Urgency**: "Last chance: [resource name] (expires [date])"
4. **Benefit**: "Save [X hours/week] with this [resource type]"
5. **Question**: "Are you making these [common mistake]?"
