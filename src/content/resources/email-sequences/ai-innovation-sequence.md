**Where should you focus your AI efforts?**

Most organizations make a critical mistake: they start with technology instead of business problems. They say "We want to use ChatGPT" instead of "We need to reduce customer support costs by 30%."

**The right approach? Start with the problem.**

Here's a simple framework:

1. **Identify a painful business problem** (high cost, poor experience, manual work)
2. **Check if AI can solve it** (pattern recognition, prediction, generation)
3. **Verify you have the data** (clean, accessible, sufficient)
4. **Assess feasibility** (skills, budget, timeline)

## Email 3: Implementation Roadmap (Day 2)

**Subject:** The 90-day AI implementation plan

**Preheader:** Go from assessment to scaled implementation

---

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

You know your readiness score. You've identified potential use cases.

Now the big question: **How do you actually implement AI?**

Most organizations try to boil the ocean. They launch 10 AI initiatives simultaneously and wonder why none of them succeed.

**The secret? Start small, measure everything, scale what works.**

Here's your 90-day roadmap:

---

### **Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)**

**Week 1: Assessment & Selection**
- Complete AI readiness assessment ✅
- Identify stakeholders and form AI team
- Select ONE pilot use case (not five)
- Define success metrics

**Week 2: Planning & Preparation**
- Map current process (what happens today)
- Define desired future state
- Identify AI tools and vendors
- Create project charter

**Week 3: Tool Setup & Testing**
- Select and configure AI tools
- Build initial prompts/workflows
- Test with small sample
- Refine based on results

**Week 4: Internal Pilot**
- Launch with pilot team
- Collect feedback daily
- Measure against success metrics
- Document learnings

---

### **Phase 2: Pilot (Days 31-60)**

**Week 5: Limited Rollout**
- Expand to small user group (5-10 people)
- Provide training and support
- Monitor performance closely
- Gather quantitative and qualitative data

**Week 6: Optimization**
- Analyze pilot data
- Identify friction points
- Optimize prompts and workflows
- Address user feedback

**Week 7: Expansion**
- Onboard additional users
- Scale successful elements
- Continue monitoring
- Build internal champions

**Week 8: Measurement & Case Study**
- Measure ROI (time saved, costs reduced, revenue impact)
- Document success story
- Present to stakeholders
- Decide: scale, pivot, or stop?

---

### **Phase 3: Scale (Days 61-90)**

**Week 9: Integration**
- Integrate AI into existing workflows
- Update SOPs and documentation
- Train broader organization
- Establish governance

**Week 10: Expansion**
- Roll out to additional teams/departments
- Identify next use cases
- Cross-pollinate learnings
- Build internal AI playbook

**Week 11: Optimization**
- Refine based on broader usage
- Automate manual handoffs
- Improve accuracy and quality
- Reduce costs

**Week 12: Review & Plan**
- Conduct post-implementation review
- Document lessons learned
- Celebrate wins (important!)
- Plan next phase

---

## **AI ROI Calculator Framework**

**Measuring AI Impact Across 3 Dimensions:**

### **1. Cost Savings**

**Time Savings:**
- Hours saved per employee/week × hourly rate × number of employees
- Example: 5 hours × $50/hour × 10 employees = $2,500/week = $130,000/year

**Labor Cost Reduction:**
- FTE hours freed up × fully loaded cost per FTE
- Example: 1 FTE × $100,000 = $100,000/year savings

**Efficiency Gains:**
- Process time reduction × volume × cost per transaction
- Example: 50% faster × 10,000 transactions × $10 = $50,000/year

---

### **2. Revenue Impact**

**Conversion Rate Improvement:**
- Additional conversions × average deal size
- Example: 50 more deals × $5,000 = $250,000 additional revenue

**Customer Lifetime Value:**
- Increased retention × CLV
- Example: 5% better retention × $10,000 CLV × 1,000 customers = $500,000

**New Revenue Opportunities:**
- New AI-powered offerings × projected adoption
- Example: $50 upsell × 1,000 customers = $50,000

---

### **3. Strategic Value** (Harder to measure but important)

**Competitive Advantage:**
- Market differentiation impact
- Time-to-market advantage
- Customer experience improvement

**Risk Reduction:**
- Compliance improvement
- Error reduction
- Security enhancement

**Employee Satisfaction:**
- Reduced manual work
- More strategic focus
- Skill development

---

## **Simple ROI Formula**

```
ROI = (Gain from Investment - Cost of Investment) / Cost of Investment × 100
```

**Example:**

**Investment:**
- AI tools: $20,000/year
- Implementation: $30,000 one-time
- Training: $10,000
- **Total: $60,000 Year 1**

**Returns:**
- Time savings: $130,000/year
- Revenue increase: $250,000/year
- **Total: $380,000 Year 1**

**ROI:**
($380,000 - $60,000) / $60,000 × 100 = **533% ROI**
