# Grounding Practices Audio Series
## 4-Part Production Scripts

---

**Professional audio scripts with timing, vocal direction, and production notes for a complete grounding practices series.**

---

## Production Guidelines

### Vocal Direction
- **Tone:** Warm, calm, inviting—not dismissive or overly cheerful
- **Pace:** Slow and deliberate. Pause longer than feels natural.
- **Volume:** Consistent, intimate (close-mic feel)
- **Style:** Conversational, like talking to a friend
- **Energy:** Grounded, centered, present

### Language Principles
- **Trauma-informed:** Invitations, not commands
- **Inclusive:** "If you'd like," "When you're ready," "If that feels okay"
- **Permission-giving:** "It's okay if," "There's no right way"
- **Validating:** Acknowledge real pain, not positive reframing

### Sound Design Notes
- **Background music:** Minimal, ambient, low volume
- **Music type:** Soft pads, gentle piano, ambient drone
- **Mix:** Music should support, not dominate
- **Pauses:** Leave 2-5 seconds of silence after key instructions
- **Transitions:** Gentle fades between sections

### Signature Lines
Use these throughout the series:
- **"If you're overwhelmed, that's not a character flaw. It's a signal."**
- **"You've survived 100% of your difficult days."**
- **"You don't need to be perfect. You need to be consistent."**

### Crisis Resources
**Mention in each audio:**
- "If you're in crisis, please reach out for support. The 988 hotline is available 24/7 by calling or texting. You don't have to do this alone."

## Part 1: EMERGENCY GROUNDING (5 minutes)

**Purpose:** For panic, overwhelm, crisis moments
**Energy:** Urgent but calm, stabilizing
**Music:** Minimal, supportive low drone

---

### [0:00-0:30] INTRO

**(Music: Low, steady drone enters softly)**

**VO (Warm, direct, calm presence):**
"If you're listening to this right now, you might be in a really hard moment. That's okay. I'm here with you.

We're going to do something very simple together. Just a few minutes to help you find your feet again.

If you're in immediate danger or thinking of hurting yourself, please call 911 or 988 right now. Those lines are open 24/7, and people are waiting to help you.

Otherwise, let's take this one breath at a time. Together."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

---

### [0:30-2:30] 5-4-3-2-1 GROUNDING

**VO:**
"I want you to look around wherever you are. If you can't see anything, that's okay too—just sense what's around you.

We're going to do a grounding exercise together. I'll guide you through it. There's no right way to do this. Just participate however you can."

**(Pause: 2 seconds)**

**VO:**
"First, find FIVE things you can see.

Just look around and name five things. In your mind, or out loud if that helps. Five things you can see..."

**(Pause: 10 seconds - allow time for listener)**

**VO:**
"You might notice a wall, a window, the color of something near you. Five things. Take your time."

**(Pause: 10 seconds)**

**VO (Gentle):**
"Good.

Now, FOUR things you can touch.

Reach out and feel four things around you. The fabric of your clothes, the surface you're sitting on, the air on your skin. Four things you can touch..."

**(Pause: 10 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Just notice the texture. The temperature. Four things."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"THREE things you can hear.

Listen. What sounds can you hear? Near or far. Traffic, voices, your own breath. Three things you can hear..."

**(Pause: 10 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Just let your ears receive whatever is here."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"TWO things you can smell.

Take a gentle breath in. Notice any smells. Coffee, soap, the air itself. Two things you can smell..."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"And ONE thing you can taste.

Or one thing you're grateful for. Or one thing that's true right now. Just one thing."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

---

### [2:30-4:00] BOX BREATHING

**VO:**
"Now let's do some breathing together. This will help your nervous system settle.

We're going to do box breathing. Four counts in, four counts hold, four counts out, four counts hold.

Ready?"

**(Pause: 2 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Inhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4.

Exhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold empty... 2, 3, 4."

**(Repeat cycle 4 times total)**

**VO (Continuing rhythm):**
"And again. Inhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4.

Exhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4.

One more time. Inhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4.

Exhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4."

**(Pause: 5 seconds after completing)**

---

### [4:00-5:00] CLOSING

**(Music: Gently begins to fade out)**

**VO (Warm, reassuring):**
"You're here. You made it through this moment.

Whatever you're feeling right now—it's okay to feel it. **If you're overwhelmed, that's not a character flaw. It's a signal.** Your system is doing exactly what it's designed to do.

This feeling will pass. All feelings do. You've survived 100% of your difficult days. This one is no different.

If you need more support, please reach out. The 988 crisis line is available anytime. You don't have to do this alone.

For now, just breathe. You're doing the best you can. And that's enough."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO (Gentle):**
"I'm glad you're here."

**(Music fades out fully over 5 seconds)**

---

## Part 2: MORNING GROUNDING (10 minutes)

**Purpose:** Start day centered and intentional
**Energy:** Gradual awakening, gentle invitation
**Music:** Soft, hopeful ambient pads

---

### [0:00-0:45] INTRO

**(Music: Soft pads, gradually present)**

**VO (Warm, greeting):**
"Good morning. I'm glad you're here.

Before you dive into your day—before the emails, the tasks, the noise—let's take a few minutes to arrive. To connect with yourself. To set an intention for how you want to be today.

This is your time. No one else's. Just you and me, for ten minutes.

Make yourself comfortable. Sit or lie down—whatever works for your body. If you need to move, that's okay too. There's no right way to do this."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

---

### [0:45-3:45] BODY CONNECTION

**VO:**
"Let's start by checking in with your body.

Your body's been with you through everything. Every challenge, every joy, every ordinary moment. Let's acknowledge that."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Gently bring your attention to the top of your head.

Just notice. What do you feel there? Any sensation? Tightness? Relaxation? Just notice without judgment."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Slowly let your attention drift down.

Through your forehead. Your eyes. Your jaw. Often we hold tension in our jaw without even realizing it. If you can, let your jaw relax just a little."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Move your awareness to your neck. Your shoulders.

If there's tension there—and there might be—just let it be there for a moment. Don't try to fix it. Just notice it."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Now, if you'd like, take a breath into that tension. And on the exhale, let your shoulders drop just an inch.

Just an inch. That's enough."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Continue scanning down.

Your arms. Your hands. Your chest. Your belly.

Notice your breath. The rise and fall. The rhythm that's been with you every moment of your life."

**(Pause: 8 seconds - allow time to notice breath)**

**VO:**
"Your hips. Your legs. Your feet.

Feel your feet touching the ground or the floor.

Whatever is supporting you right now—let yourself be supported. You don't have to hold yourself up all the way."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Your whole body, here, alive, in this moment.

Whatever your body's been through, whatever it looks like, whatever it can or can't do—it's yours. And it's kept you here. That matters."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

---

### [3:45-6:45] BREATHWORK

**VO:**
"Now let's spend a few minutes with your breath.

We're going to do a 4-7-8 breathing pattern. This helps activate your parasympathetic nervous system—your rest and digest mode.

Inhale for 4 counts. Hold for 7. Exhale for 8.

The long exhale is what signals safety to your body. So take your time with the exhale."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Ready?"

**(Pause: 2 seconds)**

**VO (Gentle pacing):**
"Inhale through your nose... 2, 3, 4.

Hold at the top... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7.

Exhale through your mouth... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8."

**(Repeat cycle 6 times total)**

**VO (Continuing):**
"And again. Inhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7.

Exhale... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8."

**(Pause: 2 seconds between cycles)**

**VO:**
"Inhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7.

Exhale... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8.

Inhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7.

Exhale... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8.

Inhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7.

Exhale... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8.

One more time. Inhale... 2, 3, 4.

Hold... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7.

Exhale... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8."

**(Pause: 8 seconds after completing)**

**VO:**
"Notice how you feel. Just a little calmer? A little more settled? Or maybe not at all, and that's okay too.

Whatever you're feeling is valid. You don't need to force any particular state."

**(Pause: 4 seconds)**

---

### [6:45-8:45] INTENTION SETTING

**VO:**
"Now, let's set an intention for your day.

An intention isn't a goal or a task. It's about how you want to BE today. What quality you want to bring forward."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Ask yourself: What do I need today?

Maybe you need patience. Maybe you need gentleness. Maybe you need courage. Maybe you need rest.

What does your body, your heart, your mind need today?"

**(Pause: 10 seconds - allow reflection)**

**VO:**
"Now, ask yourself: How do I want to show up today?

Not what you want to accomplish. But who you want to be.

Kind to yourself? Present with others? Steady in the face of challenges? Hopeful, even if things are hard?"

**(Pause: 10 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Choose one word or phrase.

Just one. Something you can return to throughout the day."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Repeat it to yourself now.

Like a quiet anchor you can carry with you. When things get stressful, when you feel overwhelmed, come back to this word."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"This is your intention. You don't have to do it perfectly. **You don't need to be perfect. You need to be consistent.** Just return to it when you remember. That's enough."

**(Pause: 4 seconds)**

---

### [8:45-10:00] CLOSING

**(Music: Gentle, hopeful uplift)**

**VO (Warm encouragement):**
"Before you go, just take one more moment to appreciate yourself.

You showed up today. You took this time for yourself. That matters.

Especially if things are hard right now, especially if you're struggling—showing up for yourself in small ways is how you get through."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"You don't know what today will bring. None of us do.

But you know who you want to be. You have an intention. You have yourself.

And that's enough. You're enough."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Whenever you're ready, carry this with you into your day.

Your breath. Your intention. Your own presence.

Take it one moment at a time. That's all any of us can do."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO (Gentle closing):**
"Have a good day. However it unfolds. You'll navigate it."

**(Music fades out over 5 seconds)**

## Part 3: MID-DAY RESET (7 minutes)

**Purpose:** Break from stress, decompression, re-centering
**Energy:** Release, relaxation, resetting
**Music:** Mid-tempo, gentle, releasing

---

### [0:00-0:30] INTRO

**(Music: Gentle but present)**

**VO (Calm, direct):**
"Pause with me for a moment.

Right here, in the middle of your day.

Whatever's happening, wherever you are, whatever you're carrying—just set it down for seven minutes.

Can you do that? Just seven minutes for yourself?"

**(Pause: 4 seconds)**

**VO:**
"You've been going. Doing. Producing. Managing.

Now, let's just be. For a few minutes.

Let your shoulders drop. Let your jaw unclench. Let yourself arrive right here."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

---

### [0:30-3:00] RELEASE TENSION

**VO:**
"Let's start by releasing some of what you're carrying.

Physically, emotionally, mentally—let's put it down for a moment."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"First, your body.

If you can, stand up. If not, sitting or lying down is fine too."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"If you're standing, give your body a little shake. Not too much. Just enough to let some tension out.

Shake your hands. Your arms. Your shoulders.

If you're sitting, roll your shoulders back a few times. Let your head drop gently side to side."

**(Pause: 10 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Let your body release what it's been holding.

You've been bracing against stress all day. Maybe without even realizing it.

Let it go. Just for now."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Now, scan your body quickly.

Where are you holding tension?

Your jaw? Your neck? Your shoulders? Your belly?"

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Without trying to fix it, just notice. 'Oh, I'm holding tension there.'

Just awareness. That's enough."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"If you'd like, take one deep breath into that tension.

And on the exhale, let it release just a little.

Not all the way. Just a little. That's progress."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Now let's release mentally.

Everything on your mind—the tasks, the worries, the shoulds, the what-ifs.

For seven minutes, set them down."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Imagine putting them on a shelf. Or in a box.

They'll be there when you get back. You don't have to solve them right now."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Just for this moment, let your mind be empty.

Let yourself be here, present, not carrying anything forward or backward.

Just now."

**(Pause: 8 seconds - spacious pause)**

---

### [3:00-5:00] PRESENT MOMENT

**VO:**
"Now let's just be here.

Right here. In this moment."

**(Pause: 4 seconds)**

**VO:**
"What can you hear right now?

Near sounds? Far sounds? The hum of a building? Birds? Traffic?

Just let your ears receive what's here."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"What can you feel?

The temperature of the air. The surface under you. The weight of your body.

Just feel. Without changing anything."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"What can you see?

Colors. Shapes. Light and shadow.

Just take it in. No analysis. Just seeing."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"This moment. Right here.

Not the next one. Not the last one.

Just this one."

**(Pause: 8 seconds - spacious)**

**VO:**
"There's nowhere else you need to be. Nothing else you need to do.

For this moment, you're exactly where you are.

And that's okay."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

---

### [5:00-6:00] RE-ORIENT

**(Music: Slight shift in energy, forward movement)**

**VO:**
"In a moment, you'll return to your day.

But before you do, let's ask: What do I need right now?

Not what you should do. What you actually need."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Do you need water? A stretch? A moment of silence? A breath?

What would actually help you in this moment?"

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Identify one small thing.

Just one. Something you can do in the next hour."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"That's your re-entry plan. One small act of care for yourself."

**(Pause: 4 seconds)**

---

### [6:00-7:00] CLOSING

**(Music: Gentle fade-out)**

**VO (Encouraging):**
"You did it. You paused.

In the middle of everything, you stopped. You checked in with yourself.

That's not small. That's huge."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Now, carry this with you.

The awareness that you can pause. The knowledge that you can reset.

You don't have to be on all the time. You don't have to carry everything at once."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"You have the rest of the day ahead of you.

You'll navigate it. Not perfectly. But you'll navigate it.

**You've survived 100% of your difficult days.** This one is no different."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO (Gentle):**
"Take one more breath. Then go, one small step at a time."

**(Music fades out over 5 seconds)**

## Part 4: EVENING INTEGRATION (10 minutes)

**Purpose:** Process day, release tension, prepare for rest
**Energy:** Winding down, reflective, restful
**Music:** Slow, warm, sleep-inducing

---

### [0:00-0:45] INTRO

**(Music: Very soft, warm, slow)**

**VO (Gentle, quiet):**
"You made it. Through another day.

However it went. However you're feeling.

You're here. At the end of it.

Let's take some time to process, to release, to prepare for rest.

This is your transition time. Between doing and being. Between the day that's passed and the night ahead."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Make yourself comfortable. Lying down might feel good. Or sitting in a way that supports rest.

Let your body settle in. Let the day begin to recede."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

---

### [0:45-3:45] DAY REFLECTION

**VO:**
"Let's briefly look back on the day.

Not to judge it. Not to fix it. Just to acknowledge it."

**(Pause: 4 seconds)**

**VO:**
"What was the hardest part of today?

What challenged you? What drained you? What felt heavy?"

**(Pause: 10 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Whatever it was, just let it be here. Acknowledge it.

'That was hard.'

You don't have to fix it right now. Just see it."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

**VO:**
"What was the best part of today?

A moment of ease? A small joy? Connection? Accomplishment?"

**(Pause: 10 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Let yourself appreciate that. Even if it was tiny.

Even if the hard outweighed the good today, there was still something. Let yourself see it."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Overall, how are you feeling as this day ends?

Exhausted? Anxious? Sad? Relieved? Grateful? Numb?"

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"However you're feeling—it's okay.

You don't have to feel different. You don't have to be positive about a hard day.

Just let yourself feel whatever is here. This is where you are."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Whatever happened today, you handled it.

Maybe not perfectly. Maybe not gracefully.

But you're here. You made it through.

That counts."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

---

### [3:45-6:45] RELEASE PRACTICE

**VO:**
"Now, let's release what you're carrying from today."

**(Pause: 4 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Imagine everything from today—the tasks, the interactions, the emotions, the stress—gathered up like a bundle."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"For tonight, set it down.

You can pick it back up tomorrow if you need to. But for now, let it go."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Physically, let's release.

Tense your whole body for a moment—every muscle, hold tight..."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"And release. Let everything drop."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Do that one more time. Tense everything—hold, hold, hold..."

**(Pause: 3 seconds)**

**VO:**
"And let go. Drop it all."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Let your breathing slow. Let your body feel heavy, supported.

Let your mind begin to quiet."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

**VO:**
"There's nothing else you need to do today.

No more tasks. No more decisions. No more performing.

Just being. Here. Resting."

**(Pause: 8 seconds - spacious, restful)**

---

### [6:45-8:45] GRATITUDE/GRACE

**VO:**
"Before we close, let's acknowledge what supported you today."

**(Pause: 4 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Who or what helped you get through today?

A person who listened? A practice that grounded you? A moment of grace you didn't expect?"

**(Pause: 10 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Let yourself feel grateful for that support.

Even in small ways. Especially in small ways."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

**VO:**
"And acknowledge yourself.

You showed up for yourself today. Maybe in big ways. Maybe in tiny ways.

But you're here. You made it to the end of this day."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

**VO:**
"That matters. You matter."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Whatever tomorrow holds, you'll navigate it.

You've navigated every day so far. Even the hard ones. Especially the hard ones."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"**You've survived 100% of your difficult days.** This one is no different.

Whatever tomorrow brings, you'll handle it. Not alone. And not without resources."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO:**
"You have practices. You have people. You have resilience you've built through every challenge you've faced."

**(Pause: 4 seconds)**

---

### [8:45-10:00] CLOSING FOR REST

**(Music: Very soft, sleep-inducing, minimal)**

**VO (Very gentle, quiet):**
"Now, let yourself rest.

Let the day go completely.

Let your body sink into whatever is supporting you.

Let your breathing slow to its natural rhythm."

**(Pause: 8 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Tomorrow is another day. You'll meet it when it comes.

For now, just tonight. Just this moment of rest."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

**VO:**
"You're doing the best you can. That's all anyone can ask.

**You don't need to be perfect. You need to be consistent.** And showing up for yourself, even in small ways, even at the end of a hard day—that's consistency."

**(Pause: 6 seconds)**

**VO:**
"Rest well. You've earned it.

Whatever tonight brings. Whatever tomorrow holds.

You'll navigate it. You always do."

**(Pause: 5 seconds)**

**VO (Fading, very quiet):**
"Goodnight. Be gentle with yourself."

**(Music fades out very slowly over 10 seconds)**

## Production Notes Summary

### Technical Specifications

**File Format:** WAV (master) → MP3/AAC (distribution)
**Sample Rate:** 44.1kHz or 48kHz
**Bit Depth:** 24-bit (master), 16-bit (distribution)
**Channels:** Mono (intimate) or Stereo (with music bed)

### Mix Guidelines

**Voice:**
- Level: -12 to -9 dB average
- Presence: Close, intimate
- Processing: Gentle compression, light EQ, no reverb (or very minimal)

**Music:**
- Level: -20 to -15 dB (well under voice)
- Frequency: Avoid competing with voice frequencies (cut mids)
- Dynamics: Consistent, gentle, no sudden changes

**Overall:**
- Loudness: -16 LUFS ( Spotify/Apple target)
- True Peak: Under -2 dBTP
- No clipping or distortion

### Recording Tips

**Voice Recording:**
- Use quality microphone (condenser or dynamic)
- Close-mic technique (3-6 inches from mouth)
- Pop filter essential
- Quiet environment (treat room if possible)
- Hydrate before recording (voice quality)

**Performance:**
- Record multiple takes
- Choose the most natural, not the most "perfect"
- Smile slightly when speaking (warms tone)
- Stand up if possible (better breath support)
- Mark pauses clearly in script

### Post-Production

**Editing:**
- Remove mistakes and long pauses
- Keep natural breaths (authenticity)
- Cut between takes if needed
- Smooth any jarring edits

**Processing Chain:**
1. EQ (High pass around 80-100 Hz, gentle presence boost 2-4 kHz)
2. Compression (Ratio 3:1, threshold -18 dB)
3. De-esser if needed (reduce sibilance)
4. Limiter (catch peaks, -1 dBTP)

**Music Integration:**
- Fade in/out gently (2-5 seconds)
- Duck music under voice if needed
- Ensure music doesn't compete with spoken words
- Use crossfades between sections

### Distribution Notes

**File Naming:**
- 01-emergency-grounding-5min.mp3
- 02-morning-grounding-10min.mp3
- 03-midday-reset-7min.mp3
- 04-evening-integration-10min.mp3

- Cover Art: [Brand-aligned image]

**Platform Considerations:**
- Spotify: Exact duration, good cover art

- App: Consider offline access

### Accessibility

**Transcript:**
- Provide full text transcript for each audio
- Include timestamp markers for sections
- Available as PDF and webpage text

**Considerations:**
- Clear articulation (especially for grounding instructions)
- Descriptive language for blind/low-vision users
- Avoid instruction that requires sight ("see this...")
- Inclusive language (not everyone can stand/stretch)

**Multi-language:**
- Consider translation if audience is international
- Dub or subtitle options for accessibility
