# Post-Setback Reflection Template
## Extract Lessons and Design Stronger Next Moves

---

**A structured template for processing setbacks, extracting wisdom, and designing your comeback.**

---

## Why Reflect on Setbacks?

Setbacks are inevitable. **What you do with them determines your trajectory.**

This template helps you:
- **Process emotions** instead of suppressing them
- **Extract lessons** instead of repeating the same patterns
- **Design better systems** instead of hoping for different results
- **Build resilience evidence** instead of eroding your self-trust

**Core principle:** Every setback contains data. Your job is to mine it.

## Quick Reference: Post-Setback Checklist

### Day 1: Feel
- [ ] Allow emotions without judgment
- [ ] Name what you're feeling
- [ ] Practice self-compassion
- [ ] Rest and regroup

### Day 2-3: Process
- [ ] Write about what happened
- [ ] Identify what you can control
- [ ] Talk to a safe person
- [ ] Begin to extract lessons

### Day 4-7: Plan
- [ ] Complete this template
- [ ] Design next steps
- [ ] Update systems
- [ ] Set new goals

### Day 8+: Move Forward
- [ ] Take action on your plan
- [ ] Track progress
- [ ] Adjust as needed
- [ ] Trust your capacity
