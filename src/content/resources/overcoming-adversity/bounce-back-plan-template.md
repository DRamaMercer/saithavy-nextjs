# Bounce-Back Plan Template
## Rebuild Routines, Goals, and Momentum After Disruption

---

**A structured template for recovering momentum and rebuilding after life knocks you off course.**

---

## About This Template

Disruptions come in many forms:
- Illness or injury
- Loss of a job or relationship
- Mental health challenges
- Family emergencies
- Unexpected life changes
- Periods of burnout or overwhelm

This template helps you rebuild thoughtfully—without rushing, shame, or the pressure to "make up for lost time."

**Core Principle:** You rebuild from where you ARE, not from where you WERE.
