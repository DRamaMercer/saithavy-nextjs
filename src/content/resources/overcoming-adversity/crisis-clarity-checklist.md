# Crisis Clarity Checklist
## Stabilize Decisions and Communication When Overwhelmed

---

**A rapid-response tool for maintaining clear thinking and effective action when everything feels chaotic.**

---

## How to Use This Checklist

**When to use:**
- You feel overwhelmed or panicked
- Multiple problems are happening at once
- You need to make important decisions under pressure
- Others are looking to you for leadership
- Communication is breaking down

**How to use:**
1. **STOP** – Put this checklist in front of you
2. **BREATHE** – Take 3 deep breaths before starting
3. **PROCEED** – Check through each section in order
4. **ACT** – Take the identified next step

**Time needed:** 5-10 minutes (worth every second)

---

## Section 1: Immediate Stabilization

### The 60-Second Reset

Before anything else, regulate your nervous system.

**Do this now:**
- [ ] **Stop moving.** Sit down. Feet on the floor.
- [ ] **Breathe.** 4 counts in, 4 counts hold, 6 counts out. Repeat 3 times.
- [ ] **Name it.** "I am in a crisis moment. I can handle this."
- [ ] **Ground.** Name 5 things you can see right now.

**My body status:**
- Heart rate: [ ] Racing [ ] Elevated [ ] Normal
- Breathing: [ ] Shallow [ ] Rapid [ ] Deep
- Hands: [ ] Shaking [ ] Clenched [ ] Steady
- Thoughts: [ ] Scrambled [ ] Spiraling [ ] Clear-ish

**If racing/elevated/shaking:** Do 5 more breaths. THEN proceed.

**Key message template:**

```
"I want to keep you informed about [situation].
Here's what's happening: [facts only]
Here's what I'm doing: [action steps]
Here's what you need to know/do: [clear instruction]
I'll update you again by: [specific time]"
```

**What NOT to do:**
- [ ] Don't speculate about what might happen
- [ ] Don't make promises you can't keep
- [ ] Don't communicate while emotional (regulate first)
- [ ] Don't ghost or disappear (even "no update yet" is an update)

## Section 7: Crisis Communication Scripts

### When You Need to Request Help

**Script:**
"Hi [name], I'm in the middle of a crisis situation with [brief description]. I need [specific request]. Can you help? I need this by [time]."

---

### When You Need to Set Boundaries

**Script:**
"I understand this is important to you. Right now I'm managing [crisis situation] and need to focus there. I'll get back to you by [specific time]."

---

### When You Don't Have Answers Yet

**Script:**
"I don't have a complete answer yet. Here's what I know: [facts]. Here's what I'm doing: [actions]. I'll have more information by [time]."

---

### When You've Made a Mistake

**Script:**
"I want to acknowledge that [mistake happened]. Here's what I'm doing to address it: [actions]. Here's my plan to prevent this in the future: [plan]. I'll update you by [time]."

---

### When You Need Time to Decide

**Script:**
"This is an important decision and I want to give it proper attention. I need [time period] to gather information and consider. I'll have an answer by [specific time]."
