## User Guide: How to Use This Template

### Getting Started (30 minutes)

**Step 1: Choose Your Format (5 min)**
- Option A: Notion template (collaborative, cloud-based)
- Option B: Excel/Google Sheets (offline capable, familiar)
- Option C: Print and fill manually (tangible, no screens)

**Step 2: Set Your Annual Theme (10 min)**
- Choose one word or phrase for 2025
- Write it on Tab 1 (Annual Overview)
- Let it guide all your decisions

**Step 3: Define Your Focus Areas (10 min)**

- Prioritize them (1=highest)
- Assign systems to each area
- Set success metrics

**Step 4: Plan Q1 in Detail (5 min)**

- Identify key milestones

**You're now ready to start!**

### Tips & Best Practices

**1. Don't Overplan**
- Leave buffer time
- Expect the unexpected
- Build in flexibility
- Less is more

**2. Theme Over Rigidity**
- Annual theme guides decisions
- Don't be hostage to specific goals
- Adjust as life happens
- Stay true to direction, flexible on tactics

**3. Regular Reviews are Non-Negotiable**
- Weekly 15-min review
- Monthly 60-min review
- Quarterly deep dive
- Annual planning ritual

**4. Celebrate Progress, Not Perfection**
- Small wins matter
- Progress over perfection
- Acknowledge growth
- Reward yourself

**5. Build in Flexibility**
- Life happens
- Plans change
- That's okay
- Adjust and keep going

**6. Use Community Accountability**
- Share your plans
- Update progress regularly
- Get support when stuck
- Celebrate together

**7. Make It Sustainable**
- Plan for your actual life
- Not your idealized life
- Build in rest
- Prevent burnout

---

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ **Planning everything in detail**
- Plan the big rocks
- Let the small pebbles fall into place
- Stay flexible on specifics

❌ **Setting too many goals**
- 3-5 goals per month max
- Quality over quantity
- Focus wins

❌ **Skipping reviews**
- Reviews are where learning happens
- Reviews enable course correction
- Reviews prevent wasted time

❌ **Being too rigid**
- Plans are guides, not laws
- Adjust as needed
- Stay true to theme, flexible on path

❌ **Not celebrating wins**
- Celebration reinforces progress
- Celebrate small wins
- Acknowledge growth

❌ **Going it alone**
- Share your plans
- Get accountability
- Join the community (Premium tier)
