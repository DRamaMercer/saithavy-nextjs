# Time-Blocking Daily Schedule Templates

**5 Templates for Different Work Styles and Schedules**

I've created these templates based on real schedules from productive remote workers. Choose the one that matches your reality, not your ideal.

---

## Template 1: Standard 9-5 Schedule

**Best for:** Traditional office hours, team coordination, consistent energy levels

```
TIME          BLOCK TYPE     ACTIVITY                  NOTES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
8:00-8:30     🟢 Shallow     Morning routine, email    Ease into day
8:30-9:00     🟢 Shallow     Plan day, prioritize      Set top 3 tasks
9:00-10:30    🔵 Deep        Priority #1              Peak focus time
10:30-10:45   🟠 Break       Walk, stretch, water      Recharge
10:45-12:00   🔵 Deep        Priority #2              Second focus block
12:00-1:00    🔴 Life        Lunch + rest              Full break
1:00-2:00     🟡 Meeting     Meetings (batched)        Grouped together
2:00-2:15     🟠 Break       Movement, snack           Reset energy
2:15-3:45     🔵 Deep        Priority #3              Afternoon focus
3:45-4:00     🟠 Break       Rest eyes, hydrate        Short break
4:00-4:30     ⚪ Buffer      Overflow, transitions     Catch-up time
4:30-5:00     🟢 Shallow     Wrap up, plan tomorrow    Review day
```

**Key Features:**
- 4.5 hours of deep work (9am-12pm, 2:15-3:45pm)
- Meetings consolidated in 1-hour block
- 15-min breaks between deep work blocks
- 30-min buffer for overflow

**Customization Tips:**
- Move deep work to morning if you peak early
- Move deep work to afternoon if you peak late
- Extend meeting block if you have more (consolidate!)
- Add 30-min exercise block if that's your priority

## Template 2: Early Bird (6am-2pm)

**Best for:** Parents, people who peak early, those who want afternoons free

```
TIME          BLOCK TYPE     ACTIVITY                  NOTES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5:30-6:00     🔴 Life        Wake up, hydrate          Quiet start
6:00-7:30     🔵 Deep        Priority #1              Peak energy
7:30-8:00     🔴 Life        Breakfast, shower         Fuel up
8:00-9:30     🔵 Deep        Priority #2              Still high energy
9:30-9:45     🟠 Break       Exercise, walk            Movement
9:45-11:15    🔵 Deep        Priority #3              Third focus block
11:15-11:30   🟠 Break       Snack, stretch            Quick recharge
11:30-12:00   🟢 Shallow     Email, Slack, admin       Wind down
12:00-1:00    🔴 Life        Lunch + rest              Full break
1:00-1:30     ⚪ Buffer      Overflow, transitions     Catch-up
1:30-2:00     🟢 Shallow     Wrap up, plan tomorrow    Review day
2:00+         🔴 Life        Free time                 Enjoy afternoon
```

**Key Features:**
- 4.5 hours of deep work before 11:30am
- Afternoons completely free (life, kids, hobbies)
- All shallow work consolidated at end
- Perfect for parents who need afternoons for family

**Real Example: Sarah, Software Engineer**
> "I shifted to 6am-2pm when my kids started school. I get 4+ hours of deep work before pickup. My output increased 40% and I'm done by 2pm every day."

**Customization Tips:**
- Start even earlier (5am) if you're truly a morning person
- Add 30-min exercise block at 9am if fitness is priority
- Shift end time to 3pm if you need more hours
- Use afternoon block for learning, not work

```
3:45-5:15     🔵 Deep        Priority #3               High focus
```

**Key Features:**

- Add 30-min nap after lunch (1:30-2pm) if that helps

## Template 4: Flexible/Async Schedule

**Best for:** Freelancers, entrepreneurs, people with variable schedules

```
ANCHOR BLOCKS (Non-negotiable)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Morning Anchor  🔵 Deep        60-90 min               Before chaos starts
Afternoon Anchor 🟢 Shallow     30-60 min               During energy dip
Evening Anchor  🟢 Shallow     30 min                  Plan tomorrow

FLEXIBLE BLOCKS (Fill in daily based on energy and obligations)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Deep Work       🔵 Deep        90-120 min blocks       Schedule 1-3 daily
Shallow Work    🟢 Shallow     30-60 min blocks        Batch together
Meetings        🟡 Meeting     30-60 min blocks        Group by type
Life            🔴 Life        Variable                Non-negotiable
Buffer          ⚪ Buffer      15-30 min               Between blocks

SAMPLE DAY (Actual usage varies daily)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
9:00-10:30     🔵 Deep        Morning anchor           Priority #1
10:30-11:00    🟠 Break       Walk, coffee
11:00-12:00    🔵 Deep        Second deep work         If energy high
12:00-1:00     🔴 Life        Lunch
1:00-2:00      🟢 Shallow     Afternoon anchor         Email, Slack, admin
2:00-3:00      🟡 Meeting     Client calls             As needed
3:00-3:30      ⚪ Buffer      Overflow, transitions
3:30-5:00      🔵 Deep        Third deep work          If needed
5:00-5:30      🟢 Shallow     Evening anchor           Plan tomorrow
```

**Key Features:**
- Only 3 non-negotiable anchor blocks
- Everything else scheduled day-of or night-before
- Adapts daily to energy and obligations
- Perfect for unpredictable schedules

**Real Example: Priya, Product Manager**
> "With two kids, my schedule is chaos. I anchor 60-90 min of deep work before they wake, 30 min of admin during nap time, and 30 min of planning after bed. Everything else is flexible. I still hit 20+ hours of deep work weekly."

**Customization Tips:**
- Move morning anchor earlier if needed (5-6am)
- Split morning anchor into two 45-min blocks if needed
- Add afternoon anchor if you have consistent downtime
- Use evening anchor for next-day planning (reduces decision fatigue)

```
Life           🔴 Life        Variable                Kid needs, meals
Buffer         ⚪ Buffer      15 min                  Transitions

SAMPLE DAY (Actual usage varies by kid schedules)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5:30-7:00      🔵 Deep        Early anchor            Before kids wake
7:00-8:00      🔴 Life        Kids + breakfast
8:00-9:00      🔵 Deep        Deep work #2            During nap/school
9:00-9:30      🟢 Shallow     Quick email check
9:30-10:00     🔴 Life        Kid break
10:00-11:00    🔵 Deep        Deep work #3            Available time
11:00-12:00    🟡 Meeting     Meetings (grouped)      If needed
12:00-1:00     🔴 Life        Lunch + kids
1:00-2:00      🟢 Shallow     Admin + Slack           Afternoon chaos
2:00-2:30      🔴 Life        Kid pickup/activities
2:30-3:30      🔵 Deep        Deep work #4            If available
3:30-4:00      ⚪ Buffer      Overflow + transitions
4:00-5:00      🔴 Life        Kids + dinner prep
5:00-5:30      🟢 Shallow     Late anchor             Plan tomorrow
5:30+          🔴 Life        Family time              Work done
```

**Key Features:**
- Maximum 60-90 min blocks (kid attention span)
- Anchors protected, everything else flexible
- Deep work split into smaller chunks
- Meetings consolidated when possible

**Real Example: Jen, Freelance Writer**
> "I used to feel guilty about not working 9-5. Now I do 60-90 min before my daughter wakes, another hour during her preschool time, and 30 min of admin after bed. I hit 3-4 hours of deep work daily and never miss school events."

**Customization Tips:**
- Shift early anchor earlier if you wake before kids (5am)
- Add midday anchor during consistent nap/quiet time
- Use evenings for shallow work only (energy is low)
- Trade off with partner if possible (each gets 2-hour anchors)

## How to Use These Templates

### Step 1: Choose Your Base Template
Pick the one that matches your current schedule, not your ideal.

### Step 2: Customize for Your Energy
- Track your energy for 3 days (rate 1-10 hourly)
- Match deep work to peak energy times (7-10 rating)
- Match shallow work to low energy times (1-4 rating)
- Match meetings to moderate energy times (4-6 rating)

### Step 3: Add Your Obligations
- Block non-negotiable meetings first
- Block life commitments (kids pickup, exercise, etc.)
- Block deep work around these, not over them

### Step 4: Add Buffers and Breaks
- Schedule 15-min buffer between major blocks
- Schedule 5-15 min breaks every 60-90 min
- Schedule 30-60 min full lunch break

### Step 5: Test for 1 Week
- Use the template as-is for 1 week
- Note what works, what doesn't
- Adjust and iterate

### Step 6: Optimize Over Time
- Review weekly (Sunday evening or Monday morning)
- Tweak based on your data
- Experiment with block durations
- Add or remove categories as needed

## Digital Calendar Setup

### Google Calendar Setup

1. **Create calendars for each category:**
   - Deep Work (blue)
   - Shallow Work (green)
   - Meetings (yellow)
   - Breaks (orange)
   - Life (red)
   - Buffer (gray)

2. **Color-code each calendar:**
   - Click the three dots next to calendar name
   - Select color
   - Apply consistently across devices

3. **Set recurring blocks:**
   - Deep work blocks: Mon-Fri, your peak hours
   - Shallow work blocks: Mon-Fri, your low-energy times
   - Breaks: Every 60-90 min during work hours
   - Weekly planning: Sunday evening or Monday morning

4. **Share with team (optional):**
   - Share Deep Work calendar as "busy"
   - Share Meeting calendar as "available"
   - Keep Life calendar private

**Pro tip:** Use Google Calendar's "Focus Time" feature for deep work blocks. It automatically declines meeting invitations.

### Notion Template Setup

1. **Create a new page**: "Daily Time Blocks"
2. **Add a table** with columns: Time, Block Type, Activity, Notes
3. **Duplicate for each workday**: Mon-Fri
4. **Add a weekly review page**: Track what worked, what didn't
5. **Add a progress tracker**: Deep work hours, priorities completed

**Pro tip:** Use Notion's database feature to track your time blocks over time and analyze patterns.
