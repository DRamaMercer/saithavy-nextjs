## Quick Start: 3 Steps to Begin

### Step 1: Choose Your Daily Template (5 min)

Pick the template that matches your current schedule:

- **Standard 9-5**: If you have set hours
- **Early bird (6am-2pm)**: If you peak in the morning
- **Night owl (12pm-8pm)**: If you come alive at night
- **Flexible/async**: If your schedule varies daily
- **Parent/break**: If you have fragmented availability

**File:** `time-blocking-daily-templates.md`

### Step 2: Complete Your Energy Audit (3 days)

Track your energy levels hourly (1-10 scale) for 3 typical workdays. Identify your peak energy hours, low energy hours, and patterns.

**File:** `time-blocking-energy-audit.md`

### Step 3: Start Time-Blocking (15 min daily)

- Morning or night before: Fill out your daily template
- Schedule deep work during peak energy hours
- Schedule shallow work during low energy hours
- Schedule meetings during moderate energy hours
- Track your metrics daily

**File:** `time-blocking-system-complete.md`
