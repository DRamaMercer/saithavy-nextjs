## Print Instructions

**For Weekly Reflection Pages**:
- Print double-sided on standard paper
- Place in a binder or folder
- Complete weekly as part of your reflection practice

**For Daily Quick-Track**:
- Print single page for each week
- Keep visible (desk, fridge, journal)
- Fill in daily (takes 30 seconds)

**For Monthly Check-Ins**:
- Print on heavier paper or cardstock
- File monthly in your growth mindset journal
- Review past months to see progress

**For Pattern Trackers**:
- Print multiple copies
- Use as needed throughout your journey
- Review monthly to identify patterns
