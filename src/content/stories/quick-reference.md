## BRAND VOICE CHEAT SHEET

### Tone
- Warm, conversational, intimate
- Vulnerable + Visionary
- Real, messy, human
- Hopeful but realistic

### What It's Not
- Heroic narratives
- Success stories dressed as vulnerability
- Polished or performed
- Toxic positivity
- Preaching or instruction

### What It Is
- Real struggle (not just telling)
- Specific examples (not generalities)
- Genuine emotion (not performed)
- Learning alongside (not teaching from above)
- "I'm in this with you" presence

## RECORDING QUICK TIPS

### Voice
- Distance: 6-8 inches from mic
- Rate: 110-130 BPM (conversational)
- Tone: Warm, present, authentic
- Emotion: Real, not performed

### Pauses
- [PAUSE 2 sec] - Breath, quick transition
- [PAUSE 5 sec] - Let point land
- [PAUSE 10 sec] - Reflection question time
- [PAUSE 15 sec] - Deeper reflection

### Emphasis
- Key phrases: Slow down 10-15%
- Important words: Slight volume increase
- Transitions: Micro-pause (0.5-1 sec)
- Signature lines: Sustain last word slightly

---

## MUSIC QUICK GUIDE

### Instrumentation
- Primarily piano
- Light strings/pads for support
- Occasional cello for emotional depth
- Never compete with voice

### Story-Specific Moods
1. **Story 1:** Intimate, hopeful
2. **Story 2:** Raw, resilient (not dark)
3. **Story 3:** Serious, courageous
4. **Story 4:** Warm, integrating

### Transitions
- Cross-fade between sections
- Match emotional tone to content
- Use subtle dynamics
- Leave space for voice

## KEY LEARNINGS BY STORY

### Story 1: Vulnerability
**Learning:** Vulnerability is strategic honesty
**Practice:** Choose one authentic moment this week
**Result:** Trust and connection

### Story 2: Failure
**Learning:** Failure is information, not identity
**Practice:** Own one failure completely
**Result:** Shame transforms to learning

### Story 3: Hard Conversations
**Learning:** Care and candor are not opposites
**Practice:** Have one avoided conversation
**Result:** Trust deepens, problems solve

### Story 4: Leading as Yourself
**Learning:** You can't lead from a place you're not inhabiting
**Practice:** Notice and stop one performance pattern
**Result:** Energy returns, leadership flows
