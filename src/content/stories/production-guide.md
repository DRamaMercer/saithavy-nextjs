# Authentic Leadership Stories - Production Guide
**Complete Audio Series Production Notes**

---

## SERIES OVERVIEW

**Title:** Authentic Leadership Stories
**Format:** 4-part audio series
**Total Duration:** 40-48 minutes (10-12 min per story)
**Target Audience:** Leaders seeking authentic presence
**Brand Voice:** Vulnerable + Visionary

### Story Sequence
1. **The Vulnerability Decision** - Choosing authenticity over protection
2. **Leading Through Failure** - Owning mistakes without collapsing
3. **The Hard Conversation** - Authenticity in difficult conversations
4. **Leading as Yourself** - Stop performing, start leading

## RECORDING SPECIFICATIONS

### Technical Requirements
- **Microphone:** High-quality condenser or dynamic mic
- **Environment:** Treated space, minimal reverb
- **Distance:** 6-8 inches from mic (close, intimate sound)
- **Format:** WAV, 48kHz, 24-bit
- **Post-Production:** Light compression, EQ for warmth

### Voice Direction
- **Speaking Rate:** 110-130 BPM (conversational)
- **Tone:** Warm, present, authentic
- **Emotion:** Genuine emotion, not performed emotion
- **Pauses:** Natural, not rigid - leave space for meaning to land

### Emphasis Technique
- **Key Phrases:** Slow down by 10-15%
- **Important Words:** Slight volume increase (+2-3dB)
- **Transitions:** Micro-pause (0.5-1 second)
- **Reflection Questions:** Sustain last word slightly

## MUSIC DIRECTION

### Story 1: The Vulnerability Decision
- **Opening:** Soft, contemplative piano intro
- **Story:** Ambient piano texture, no melody
- **Learning:** Light strings/pad, slight uplift
- **Reflection:** Minimal music, harmonic swells
- **Closing:** Gentle piano, hopeful resolution

### Story 2: Leading Through Failure
- **Opening:** Sparse, melancholic but not dark
- **Story:** Piano + subtle cello, serious
- **Learning:** Add gentle rhythm, forward-moving
- **Reflection:** Mostly voice, occasional support
- **Closing:** Warm but realistic, not false cheer

### Story 3: The Hard Conversation
- **Opening:** Low notes, sparse piano, tension without drama
- **Story:** Low, contemplative, subtle harmonic tension
- **Learning:** Release tension, add warmth and forward movement
- **Reflection:** Minimal, focused on voice
- **Closing:** Resolve with warmth and strength

### Story 4: Leading as Yourself
- **Opening:** Warm, reflective piano, more melody
- **Story:** Warm piano + light hopeful strings
- **Learning:** Gentle momentum, subtle rhythm
- **Reflection:** Minimal, warm piano
- **Closing:** Swell gently, fade with warmth

### Music Production Notes
- **Instrumentation:** Primarily piano, light strings/cello, occasional pads
- **Tempo:** Slow to moderate (60-80 BPM)
- **Dynamics:** Subtle, never competing with voice
- **Transitions:** Cross-fade sections, no jarring changes

## PAUSE MARKING SYSTEM

### Standard Pauses
- **[PAUSE 2 seconds]** - Breath, quick transition
- **[PAUSE 3-5 seconds]** - Let point land, brief reflection
- **[PAUSE 10 seconds]** - Reflection question processing time
- **[PAUSE 15 seconds]** - Deeper reflection, multiple questions

### Pause Placement
- After key insights (let meaning land)
- Before transitions (create space)
- In reflection sections (processing time)
- Before signature lines (emphasis)

### Recording Technique
- Count silently in head
- Don't rush the pause
- Let the silence work
- Maintain presence during silence
