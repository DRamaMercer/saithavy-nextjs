### Part 3: Deep Work Practice (12-15 minutes)

**Key Concepts:**
- The 5 phases of deep work
- Transition zone discomfort (normal, not a sign to stop)
- Distraction handling system
- Deep work as practice, not performance

**What You'll Learn:**
- How to enter deep work consistently (even when you don't feel like it)
- How to handle distractions when they arise
- The complete 90-minute deep work cycle
- How to complete a deep work session properly

**Practice Introduced:**
- Mini 5-minute deep work session (full walkthrough)
  - Setup ritual
  - Commitment statement
  - Begin work
  - Check-in
  - Complete and transition

**The 5 Phases:**
1. **Setup (2 min)** - Clear space, phone away, open only what you need
2. **Commitment (1 min)** - Say out loud what you're committing to
3. **Transition (10-15 min)** - The discomfort zone, push through
4. **Deep Work (60-70 min)** - You're in the zone, stay in it
5. **Exit (5-10 min)** - Complete thought, write down where you left off

**Distraction Handling System:**
- NOTICE: "Oh, distraction arising"
- NAME: "That's the urge to check my phone"
- WRITE: Jot it down, don't act on it
- RETURN: Immediately, without judgment

**Real Vulnerability:**
- Deep work is supposed to be hard
- Host still feels resistance every time
- Some days are struggles, not failures

### Part 4: Sustaining Focus Long-Term (12-15 minutes)

**Key Concepts:**
- Focus as a trainable skill (4 aspects)
- Energy management vs. time management
- Recovery as essential (3 types)
- 30-day focus challenge

**What You'll Learn:**
- How to manage energy, not just time
- The hidden key to sustainable deep work: recovery
- How to work with your natural rhythms
- A concrete 30-day plan to build lasting focus

**Challenge Introduced:**
**30-Day Focus Challenge:**
- Week 1: 30 minutes/day (foundation)
- Week 2: 60 minutes/day (building)
- Week 3: 90 min OR 2x60 min (stretching)
- Week 4: Find your sustainable rhythm (integration)

**Focus as a Skill:**
1. **Trainable** - Neuroplasticity, you can build it
2. **Plateaus and breakthroughs** - Don't quit during plateaus
3. **Requires maintenance** - Train + rest (like a muscle)
4. **Context-dependent** - Varies based on sleep, stress, etc.

**Recovery Types:**
1. **Micro-Recovery** (5-15 min) - Between sessions
2. **Mid-Recovery** (30-90 min) - After morning deep work
3. **Deep Recovery** (evening/weekend) - Prevent burnout

**Energy Management:**
- Morning (peak): 3-4 hours deep focus capacity
- Midday (recovery): Real break, away from screens
- Afternoon (moderate): Meetings, email, shallow work
- Evening (rest): No deep work, personal time

## FAQ

**Do I need to listen in order?**
Yes. Each episode builds on the previous one. Start with Part 1.

**How long until I see results?**
Most people notice a difference within the first week. Significant transformation happens over 2-4 weeks of consistent practice.

**What if I miss a day during the 30-day challenge?**
That's okay. The goal is consistency over time, not perfection. Just pick up where you left off.

**Can I do the challenge if I have a variable schedule?**
Absolutely. The system is designed to be adaptable to YOUR life and energy patterns.

**Do I need special equipment?**
No. Just a quiet space and something to take notes with. The focus system works with any setup.

**Is this just for knowledge workers?**
The principles apply to anyone who needs to do focused work - students, creatives, professionals, entrepreneurs.
