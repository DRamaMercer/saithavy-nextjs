## Core Frameworks Taught

### The 3 Types of Focus
1. **Shallow Focus** - Email, Slack, routine tasks (all day)
2. **Moderate Focus** - Sustained attention on medium-complexity (2-3 hours)
3. **Deep Focus** - Complete concentration on demanding tasks (90 min max)

### The 5 Phases of Deep Work
1. **Setup** (2 min) - Clear space, phone away
2. **Commitment** (1 min) - Say it out loud
3. **Transition** (10-15 min) - Push through resistance
4. **Deep Work** (60-70 min) - You're in the zone
5. **Exit** (5-10 min) - Complete thought, write where you left off

### Distraction Handling
**Notice → Name → Write → Return**

### The 3 Types of Recovery
1. **Micro-Recovery** (5-15 min) - Between sessions
2. **Mid-Recovery** (30-90 min) - After morning deep work
3. **Deep Recovery** (evening/weekend) - Prevent burnout

### 30-Day Focus Challenge
- **Week 1:** 30 min/day (foundation)
- **Week 2:** 60 min/day (building)
- **Week 3:** 90 min OR 2x60 (stretching)
- **Week 4:** Find your rhythm (integration)
