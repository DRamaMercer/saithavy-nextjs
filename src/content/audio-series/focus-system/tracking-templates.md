## Usage Notes

**How to use these templates:**

1. **Start simple** - Just track session duration and quality at first
2. **Add detail gradually** - Add energy tracking, distraction logging as you establish the habit
3. **Weekly review** - Take 10 minutes each week to review patterns
4. **Monthly reflection** - Use monthly tracker to see longer-term trends
5. **Adjust as needed** - These are templates - customize for YOUR needs

**Key metrics to track:**
- Session duration and consistency
- Quality ratings (subjective but useful)
- Energy levels (correlation with focus)
- Distraction patterns (what pulls you away)
- Recovery quality (rest is essential)

**Remember:** The goal isn't perfect tracking. The goal is awareness and pattern recognition. Track enough to learn, not so much that it becomes a burden.
