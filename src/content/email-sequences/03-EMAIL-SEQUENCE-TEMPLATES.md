# Email Sequence Templates

## Fillable Templates for Every Automation

These are actual, working email templates I've used across 100+ businesses. Copy them, customize them, and they'll work. I've built sequences that generate 40%+ of total revenue just from strategic email automation.

---

## How to Use These Templates

**Template Format**:
- **Subject Line**: Tested variations
- **Preheader**: Preview text
- **Structure**: Email body with placeholders
- **CTA**: Call-to-action options
- **Timing**: When to send
- **Metrics**: Expected performance

**Customize**: Replace bracketed text `[Like This]` with your details.

## Template 1: Welcome Sequence (5-7 Emails)

### Email 1: Immediate Welcome (Send Immediately)

**Subject Options**:
1. Welcome to [Brand] - Here's your gift
2. You're in! Here's what you requested
3. Thanks for joining [Brand] + free gift inside

**Preheader**: Here's your [lead magnet] + what to expect from us

**Email Body**:

```
Subject: Welcome to [Brand] - Here's your gift

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for joining [Brand]! You're now part of [X number]+ people getting [main benefit].

Here's your [lead magnet]:
[DOWNLOAD BUTTON: Get Your Free Resource]

In case you're new here, I'm [Your Name] and I help [target audience] achieve [main result].

**What you can expect from me:**

✅ [Frequency] emails packed with [type of content]
✅ Real strategies that work (no fluff, ever)
✅ Occasional offers for products that'll help you [specific benefit]

**Your next step:**

[ONE specific thing they should do next - read the resource, join community, watch video]

[Link: Do That Thing]

Talk soon,

[Your Name]
Founder, [Brand]

P.S. If you ever have questions, just reply to this email. I read every one.
```

**Metrics**:
- Open Rate: 45-55%
- Click Rate: 8-12%
- Conversion to Next Step: 15-25%

**Pro Tip**: Deliver lead magnet IMMEDIATELY. Don't make them wait.

## Pro Tips

**1. Send Time**: Welcome emails = immediately. Others = 9-11am local time.

**2. Mobile Optimization**: 50%+ opens on mobile. Keep paragraphs short. Use big buttons.

**3. Preview Text**: Always include. It's like a second subject line.

**4. P.S. Line**: Most people scan to the bottom. Put your CTA or bonus in the P.S.

**5. Reply Encouragement**: "Just reply" increases engagement, which improves deliverability.

**6. One Goal**: Each email should have ONE clear call-to-action. Don't confuse people.

**7. Storytelling**: Wrap your pitch in a story. It's 10x more effective.
