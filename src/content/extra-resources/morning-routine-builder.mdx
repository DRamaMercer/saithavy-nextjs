## Why Morning Routines Matter

Research shows that intentional mornings:
- **Boost willpower** for the entire day
- **Reduce decision fatigue** and stress
- **Increase focus** and productivity
- **Improve mood** and wellbeing

## Building Your Routine

### Step 1: Assess Your Current Morning
- Time available evaluation
- Energy pattern identification
- Current habit audit
- Goal alignment check

### Step 2: Choose Your Foundation
- Select activities that energize you
- Match routine length to your schedule
- Align with your leadership values
- Ensure realistic sustainability

### Step 3: Stack and Simplify
- Use habit stacking for automaticity
- Start simple, build complexity gradually
- Create backup options for busy days
- Remove friction points

### Step 4: Track and Iterate
- Monitor your energy and mood
- Adjust based on real results
- Celebrate small wins
- Build in flexibility
