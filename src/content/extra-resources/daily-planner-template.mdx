## How to Use This Template

### Morning Ritual (10 Minutes)

Before checking email or opening Slack, complete the morning section:

1. **Set Your Top 3 Priorities** - What would make today a success?
2. **Schedule Your Focus Blocks** - Protect your peak energy hours
3. **Plan Your Breaks** - Schedule movement, meals, and mental breaks
4. **Set Your Intention** - What mindset will serve you today?

### Throughout the Day

- Use the **Time-Blocking Grid** to map out your schedule
- Mark your **Energy Levels** hourly to identify patterns
- Log **Distractions** as they occur to build awareness
- Check off **Completed Tasks** to build momentum

### Evening Review (10 Minutes)

Before closing your laptop:

1. **Review Your Wins** - What went well today?
2. **Identify Learnings** - What would you do differently?
3. **Rate Your Energy** - Track your overall energy management
4. **Plan Tomorrow** - Set priorities before leaving your workspace
