## How This Journal Transforms Your Leadership

### Daily Practice (15 Minutes)

Each day, answer three targeted prompts:

**Morning Prompt:**
> "What leadership challenge am I facing today, and what mindset will serve me best?"

**Midday Check-In:**
> "How am I showing up for my team right now? Am I leading from reactivity or intention?"

**Evening Reflection:**
> "What did I learn about myself as a leader today? What would I do differently?"

### Weekly Deep-Dive

Each week, complete a comprehensive reflection:

1. **Review Your Week** - Patterns, wins, and growth areas
2. **Examine Your Decisions** - Were they aligned with your values?
3. **Analyze Your Emotions** - What triggered you? What energized you?
4. **Set Your Intention** - How will you grow next week?

### Monthly Leadership Audit

Use the structured framework to:

- Evaluate your leadership growth across 6 dimensions
- Identify blind spots and growth opportunities
- Celebrate progress and recalibrate your development plan
- Get clarity on your leadership philosophy and values

When you understand yourself, you better understand others. Develop:

- Empathy for your team's challenges and motivations

- Lead from your strengths, not weaknesses
- Build resilience through intentional recovery
