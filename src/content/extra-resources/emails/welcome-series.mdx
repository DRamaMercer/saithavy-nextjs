## Email 2: Day 3 - The One Thing

**Subject:** The one thing that changes everything

---

Hey!

Quick question:

What's the difference between people who thrive under pressure and those who crumble?

It's not talent. It's not luck. It's not even experience.

**It's emotional regulation.**

The ability to pause between stimulus and response. To feel the feelings without being controlled by them. To choose your actions instead of reacting.

Here's the truth nobody tells you:

**Regulation is a skill.**

Not a personality trait. Not something you're born with. It's something you practice.

And like any skill, you get better at it with practice.

This week, try this:

Notice when you feel triggered. Just notice. Don't change anything yet.

Watch what happens in your body. Where do you feel it? What thoughts come up?

**That's the first step.**

We'll build from there.

See you Wednesday,

Saithavy

## Email 3: Day 7 - Your First Step

**Subject:** Your first step

---

Hey!

So you've been noticing your triggers this week.

How'd it go?

Maybe you noticed tension in your shoulders when your boss sent that email. Or that pit in your stomach before that meeting. Or that urge to scroll when you should be working.

**That's awareness.**

And awareness is where change starts.

Here's your first action step:

Choose ONE technique from the Calm Under Pressure Playbook. Just one.

Maybe it's box breathing. Maybe it's the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding. Maybe it's the pressure pivot.

Practice it this week. Not when you're calm—when you need it.

That's how it becomes automatic.

**Your regulated life starts with one breath.**

Let's go,

Saithavy
