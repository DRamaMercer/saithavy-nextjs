## Technical Specifications

### Video Requirements

**Essential Specs:**
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical only)
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 minimum (recommended: 4K)
- **Duration**: 15-60 seconds (sweet spot: 21-34 seconds)
- **Frame Rate**: 24fps, 30fps, or 60fps
- **File Format**: MP4 or MOV (H.264 codec)
- **File Size**: Under 500MB (recommended: under 100MB for fast upload)

**Audio Specifications:**
- **Audio Quality**: 44.1kHz, 128kbps minimum
- **Music**: Use trending sounds or copyright-free
- **Voice Volume**: -12dB to -6dB (dialogue should be clear)
- **Music Volume**: -18dB to -12dB (background, not overpowering)

### Text & Captions

**Character Limits:**
- **Caption**: 0-150 characters (optimal: 80-120)
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 hashtags max (more looks spammy)
- **Text Overlays**: Maximum 20 characters per line

**Text Overlay Guidelines:**
- Font size: Minimum 40pt for readability
- Position: Center 50-70% of screen (avoid UI overlays)
- Duration: Text must be visible for minimum 2 seconds
- Color: High contrast with background
- Style: On-brand, consistent, legible

## Content Structure Template

### The 3-Act TikTok Structure

```
[0-3 seconds] HOOK - Visual + Audio grab
├─ Visual shock element
├─ Pattern interrupt
├─ Bold statement or question
└─ Trending sound or original beat

[3-20 seconds] VALUE - Core content delivery
├─ One clear idea (don't cram multiple)
├─ Fast-paced editing
├─ Text overlays for key points
└─ Maintain energy throughout

[20-60 seconds] CTA - Clear call to action
├─ Specific ask (follow, like, comment)
├─ Reward for action (e.g., "Part 2 coming")
├─ Create urgency or FOMO
└─ End on high note
```

## Hook Templates

### Visual Shock Hooks

```

```


## Algorithm Optimization

### Key Ranking Signals

1. **Watch Time** - Most important metric
   - Aim for 100% completion rate
   - Rewatches signal high quality

2. **Engagement Rate** - Interactions relative to views
   - Comments > Shares > Likes
   - Focus on sparking conversation

3. **Completion Rate** - Viewers watch full video
   - Hook in first 0-3 seconds critical
   - Maintain energy throughout

4. **Session Time** - How long viewer stays on platform
   - Create binge-worthy content
   - Encourage watching more of your content

### Content Categorization

```
Help your content get categorized correctly:

1. Use consistent hashtags
2. Stay within your niche (don't pivot randomly)
3. Use trending sounds (when relevant)
4. Engage with similar creators
5. Post consistently at optimal times
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Weak hooks** - First 3 seconds determine 80% of success
2. **Too long** - Over 60 seconds hurts completion rate
3. **No CTA** - Every video should have a purpose
4. **Poor audio** - Bad audio = immediate scroll
5. **Inconsistent posting** - Algorithm rewards consistency
6. **Ignoring trends** - Trending content gets boost
7. **Text over UI** - Keep text in safe zone (center 50-70%)
8. **Buying views/likes** - Can shadowban your account
