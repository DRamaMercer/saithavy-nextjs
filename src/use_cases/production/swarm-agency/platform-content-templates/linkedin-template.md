## Format Specifications

### Document Posts

**Technical Specs:**
- **Format**: PDF upload
- **Pages**: No limit (optimal: 1-5 pages)
- **File Size**: Under 100MB (recommended: under 5MB)
- **Text**: Extractable (LinkedIn shows preview text)
- **Cover Image**: 1599x898 pixels recommended

**When to Use:**
- In-depth guides and frameworks
- Case studies and white papers
- Research findings and reports
- Slide decks from presentations
- Templates and worksheets

**Best Practices:**
- Include executive summary in post
- Add compelling cover image
- Design for mobile viewing
- Include clear CTA at end
- Make it visually appealing

### Standard Posts

**Character Limits:**
- **Text**: 3,000 characters maximum
- **Headline**: First 1-2 lines critical (show without "see more")
- **Optimal Length**: 1,000-1,500 characters (3-5 paragraphs)
- **Spacing**: Use line breaks for readability

**Media:**
- **Images**: Up to 9 images
- **Videos**: Up to 10 minutes, 5GB
- **Documents**: PDF only

### Video Posts

**Technical Specs:**
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:2.4 (vertical) or 16:9 (horizontal) or 1:1 (square)
- **Resolution**: 720p minimum, 1080p recommended
- **Duration**: Under 10 minutes (optimal: 1-5 minutes)
- **File Size**: Up to 5GB
- **File Format**: MP4, MOV, AVI

**Video Guidelines:**
- Professional quality expected
- Captions recommended (auto-generated or manual)
- Thumbnail important (first frame)
- First 3 seconds critical for engagement

### Polls

**Specs:**
- **Duration**: 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, or 2 weeks
- **Options**: 2 or 4 choices
- **Characters per option**: Up to 140 characters

**When to Use:**
- Market research
- Community feedback
- Engagement boost
- Trend identification

**Poll Formula:**
```
Question: [Clear, specific question]
Option 1: [Choice 1]
Option 2: [Choice 2]
Option 3: [Choice 3]
Option 4: [Choice 4]

Caption: Why I'm asking this...
[I'll share results in comments]
```

## Hashtag Strategy

### LinkedIn Hashtag Formula (3-5 per post)

```markdown
1. **Broad Category** (1 hashtag)
   - Example: #leadership, #marketing, #technology

2. **Specific Niche** (1-2 hashtags)
   - Example: #digitalmarketing, #productivitytips, #careergrowth

3. **Trending/Community** (1-2 hashtags, optional)
   - Example: #motivationmonday, #technews

Total: 3-5 hashtags per post maximum
```

### Hashtag Best Practices

```
DO:
✓ Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
✓ Mix broad + specific
✓ Place at end of post
✓ Research hashtag popularity (aim for 10K-100K followers)

DON'T:
✗ Use more than 5 hashtags (looks spammy)
✗ Use irrelevant hashtags
✗ Use only super broad hashtags (millions of followers)
✗ Use hashtags in every sentence
```

## Algorithm Optimization

### Key Ranking Signals (2025)

1. **Dwell Time** (Most Important)
   - Time spent reading post
   - "See more" clicks signal quality

2. **Engagement Rate**
   - Comments > Shares > Likes
   - Meaningful comments (not just emojis)
   - Conversation threads

3. **Recency**
   - Posts have 24-48 hour lifespan
   - Post when audience is active

4. **Author Authority**
   - Your expertise in the topic
   - Consistency in posting
   - Engagement with your own content

5. **Content Relevance**
   - Stay in your niche
   - Build topical authority
   - Don't pivot randomly

### Content Categorization

```
Help LinkedIn categorize your content:

1. Post consistently on specific topics
2. Use relevant keywords (not just hashtags)
3. Engage with content in your niche
4. Create content series (builds authority)
5. Use LinkedIn's topic tags (when available)
```

## Newsletter Strategy

### LinkedIn Newsletter Benefits

```
✓ Sends notification to all followers when published
✓ Appears in dedicated newsletter feed
✓ Higher reach than regular posts
✓ Builds subscriber list
✓ Positions you as authority
```

### Newsletter Best Practices

```
1. Consistent schedule (weekly or bi-weekly)
2. Compelling title (headline)
3. Valuable content (not just promotional)
4. Readable length (1,000-1,500 words)
5. Clear CTA (what should reader do)
6. Personal voice (your perspective)
```

50K+ connections: 1-2% engagement rate

Engagement Rate Formula:
((reactions + comments + shares) / impressions) × 100

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Weak hooks** - First 1-2 lines determine if people click "see more"
2. **Too long** - Over 1,500 characters hurts engagement
3. **Too promotional** - Provide value first, promote second
4. **Poor formatting** - Use line breaks and spacing
5. **Irrelevant hashtags** - Use only 3-5 relevant tags
6. **Ignoring comments** - Engagement is everything
7. **Inconsistent posting** - Algorithm rewards consistency
8. **Being too formal** - LinkedIn rewards authenticity
