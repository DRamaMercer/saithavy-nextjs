# Platform Health Dashboard Template

*Comprehensive dashboard for tracking platform health scores, content performance, underperformance analysis, and resource allocation recommendations.*

---

## Purpose

This template provides a structured format for:

1. **Platform Health Scores** - Overall health metrics per platform
2. **Top Performing Content** - Identify and analyze winners
3. **Underperformance Analysis** - Diagnose and fix poor performers
4. **Resource Allocation** - Data-driven budget and time recommendations
5. **Growth Opportunities** - Identify expansion potential
6. **Risk Assessment** - Monitor platform-specific risks
