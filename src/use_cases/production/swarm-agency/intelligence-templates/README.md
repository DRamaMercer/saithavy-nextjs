# Intelligence Templates

*Weekly reporting and competitive intelligence templates for tracking platform updates, trends, and competitor analysis.*

---

## Overview

This directory contains templates for:

1. **Weekly Intelligence Brief** - Comprehensive weekly update on all platforms
2. **Competitive Intelligence Report** - Competitor analysis and benchmarking

---

## Templates

### Weekly Intelligence Brief (`weekly-intelligence-brief.md`)

**Purpose**: Structured weekly report covering platform algorithm updates, emerging trends, competitive intelligence, and action items.

**What It Tracks**:
- **Platform Algorithm Updates** - Changes across all platforms
- **Emerging Trends** - New content trends and formats
- **Competitive Intelligence** - What competitors are doing
- **Our Performance Review** - Top and underperforming content
- **Action Items by Swarm** - Tasks for each team
- **Strategy Adjustments** - Immediate, short-term, and long-term
- **Resource Allocation** - Time and budget recommendations

**When to Use**:
- Every week (consistent schedule)
- After major algorithm changes
- When launching new campaigns
- Before strategy planning sessions

**How to Use**:
1. Fill out sections throughout the week as information comes in
2. Complete review sections at week's end
3. Distribute to relevant stakeholders
4. Use to inform next week's content calendar

**Sections Included**:
- Executive Summary
- Platform Algorithm Updates (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, etc.)
- Emerging Trends (content, format, hashtags)
- Competitive Intelligence (competitor analysis)
- Our Performance Review (top/underperforming content)
- Action Items by Swarm
- Strategy Adjustments
- Resource Allocation Recommendations

---

### Competitive Intelligence Report (`competitive-intelligence-report.md`)

**Purpose**: Focused analysis of competitor strategies and performance.

**What It Tracks**:
- **Competitor Overview** - Key competitors in your space
- **Content Analysis** - What's working for them
- **Engagement Strategies** - How they build community
- **Growth Tactics** - How they're growing
- **Benchmarking** - Industry standards and comparisons

**When to Use**:
- Monthly deep-dive into competitors
- When planning new campaigns
- Before strategy pivots
- Quarterly competitive reviews

**How to Use**:
1. Identify top 5-10 competitors
2. Track their content consistently
3. Analyze top-performing content
4. Identify patterns and strategies
5. Extract actionable insights

## Best Practices

### 1. Consistency

- Publish brief on same day each week
- Use consistent format and structure
- Track same competitors over time
- Maintain consistent metrics

### 2. Timeliness

- Update algorithm updates as they happen
- Note trends early (first-mover advantage)
- Review competitor content weekly
- Distribute brief quickly after week ends

### 3. Actionability

- Every trend should have action items
- Every competitor insight should inform strategy
- Every algorithm update should have response plan
- Every underperformance should have corrective action

### 4. Distribution

- Share with relevant team members
- Archive for future reference
- Use in strategy planning sessions
- Create actionable summaries

## Section Breakdown

### Executive Summary

**Purpose**: Quick overview of most important updates

**What to Include**:
- Critical algorithm updates
- Top opportunities
- Key risks
- Performance highlights

**Time to Complete**: 15 minutes

---

### Platform Algorithm Updates

**Purpose**: Track changes that affect content strategy

**Platforms Covered**:
- TikTok
- Instagram
- YouTube
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn
- Pinterest
- Facebook

**For Each Update**:
- Date identified
- Source of information
- Severity level
- What changed
- Implications
- Recommended actions

**Time to Complete**: 30-45 minutes

---

### Emerging Trends

**Purpose**: Identify new content opportunities early

**Trend Categories**:
- Content trends (what formats are working)
- Format trends (new ways to present content)
- Hashtag trends (what's being discovered)
- Keyword trends (search behavior changes)

**For Each Trend**:
- First identified
- Trend stage (emerging/growing/peaking/declining)
- Estimated lifespan
- Our opportunity
- Action items

**Time to Complete**: 30 minutes

---

### Competitive Intelligence

**Purpose**: Learn from competitors' successes and failures

**For Each Competitor**:
- Platform presence
- Growth metrics
- What's working
- What we can learn
- Action items

**Time to Complete**: 45-60 minutes

---

### Our Performance Review

**Purpose**: Analyze our own content performance

**Sections**:
- Top performing content (what worked)
- Underperforming content (what didn't)
- Platform performance summary
- Key learnings

**Time to Complete**: 30-45 minutes

---

### Action Items by Swarm

**Purpose**: Assign tasks to appropriate teams

**Swarm Categories**:
- Content Creation Swarm
- Design & Editing Swarm
- Analytics & Optimization Swarm

**For Each Action**:
- Task description
- Priority level
- Assigned owner
- Due date
- Status

**Time to Complete**: 15-20 minutes

## Action Item Priority Levels

### High Priority
- Algorithm changes requiring immediate action
- Viral trends (time-sensitive)
- Underperforming content fixes
- Crisis management

### Medium Priority
- Emerging trends (not time-critical)
- Optimization opportunities
- Testing new formats
- Competitive responses

### Low Priority
- Long-term strategy considerations
- Nice-to-have improvements
- Exploratory projects
- Research and learning

## Quick Start

### Week 1 Setup

1. **Create master document** - Copy template
2. **Set up tracking** - Identify competitors to monitor
3. **Schedule updates** - Block time on calendar
4. **Set up alerts** - Platform update notifications
5. **Distribution list** - Identify who receives brief

### Weekly Workflow

**Monday**: Set up template, note weekend trends
**Tuesday-Wednesday**: Update as information comes in
**Thursday**: Complete competitor analysis
**Friday**: Finish performance review, distribute
