---

## Understanding Beginnings

**William Bridges teaches that new beginnings have two parts:**
1. **External beginning:** The new situation, role, or circumstance
2. **Internal beginning:** The psychological shift, new identity, fresh start

**Most people jump to the external beginning** (new job, new house, new relationship) without doing the internal beginning work (who am I now? what do I want? how do I want to be?).

**This workbook is for the internal beginning.** Designing who you want to be and how you want to live in this next chapter.

**Timing matters:** You can't rush this. Beginnings happen when:
- The ending feels complete
- The neutral zone has done its work
- Clarity starts to emerge
- Energy returns
- You feel ready (even if nervous)

**If you're not ready, that's okay.** Return to Document 3 (Navigating The Neutral Zone) and give yourself more time.

## Part 5: What Does Success Look Like?

### Redefine Success

**In this chapter, what does success mean?** (Not society's definition, YOURS)

**Success is NOT:**
- What others expect of you
- Keeping up with appearances
- Doing it all perfectly
- Never struggling
- Having it figured out

- Living aligned with your values
- Being honest with yourself
- Taking small steps forward

## Part 6: Beginning Rituals

### Mark The Beginning

**Just as you honored the ending, honor the beginning.** Create a ritual that marks this new chapter.

**Choose one or more:**

**1. The Declaration Ritual**
- Write a declaration of who you're becoming
- Read it aloud to someone you trust
- Post it where you'll see it daily

**2. The Symbol Ritual**
- Choose an object that represents your new chapter
- Wear it, carry it, or display it
- Let it remind you of who you're becoming

**3. The Action Ritual**
- Take one symbolic action that represents your beginning
- Example: First day in new office, first workout, first creative session
- Make it meaningful, not just functional

**4. The Sharing Ritual**
- Tell someone you trust about your new chapter
- Share your commitments and intentions
- Ask them to witness and support you

**5. The Release Ritual**
- Release one last thing from the old chapter
- Example: Delete an old app, donate old clothes, clear a space
- Make room for what's new

## Part 8: Ongoing Alignment

### Weekly Check-In

**Every week for the next 90 days, ask:**

1. **Who am I being this week?** (Does it align with who I want to be?)
2. **What values am I honoring?** (What am I neglecting?)
3. **What practices am I keeping?** (What have I dropped?)
4. **What needs adjustment?** (What's not working?)
5. **What's one small step I can take?** (Forward movement)

### Monthly Review

**Every month, reflect:**

1. **What's different about this month compared to last month?**
2. **What am I proud of?**
3. **What challenged me?**
4. **What am I learning about myself?**
5. **What do I want to focus on next month?**

### Quarterly Reset

**Every 3 months, reassess:**

1. **Is this still the chapter I want to be in?**
2. **What's working? What's not?**
3. **What needs to change?**
4. **What's the next 90-day focus?**

## Moving Forward

**You've designed your beginning.** Now:

1. **Do Document 5: Transition Support Plan** (Build your ongoing support system)
2. **Begin taking small steps** (Consistent small actions create transformation)
3. **Track your progress** (Celebrate wins, learn from setbacks)

**Every season is training.** This new chapter is your opportunity to live into who you're becoming.
