# Recovery Protocol Guide
## Because Recovery Is Productive (Not Wasted Time)

**The truth I learned the hard way:** I treated recovery like a reward - something I earned after working hard. That's backwards. Recovery is how I sustain my capacity to work hard. Without it, I don't just stop being productive - I become a shadow of myself.

---

## Recovery Protocol Checklist

**Before committing to new activities, ask:**
- ☐ Does this replenish or deplete my energy?
- ☐ Do I have recovery capacity available?
- ☐ What will I recover with after this?
- ☐ Am I saying yes or hell yes?

**If it doesn't replenish, defer.**
