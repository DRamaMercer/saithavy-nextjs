# Relationship Inventory
## Mapping Your Key Connections

**Welcome to your relationship inventory.** I created this tool after realizing I was "managing" my relationships the same way I managed my calendar - as logistics to coordinate, not living systems to nurture. I had people I "should" catch up with, relationships I was neglecting, and connections draining me I wouldn't address for years.

This inventory isn't about optimizing your network. It's about seeing your relationships clearly so you can invest where it matters.

## Part 3: Assessment Key

**Connection Quality (1-10):**
- 1-3: Strained, transactional, or draining
- 4-6: Functional but not fulfilling
- 7-8: Strong, mutually beneficial
- 9-10: Exceptional, growth-oriented, deeply aligned

**Energy Exchange:**
- **Giving more:** You invest more energy than you receive
- **Receiving more:** You receive more than you contribute
- **Balanced:** Energy flows both ways relatively equally
- **Draining:** The relationship consistently depletes you
- **Fueling:** The relationship consistently energizes you

**Growth Potential:**
- **Declining:** Relationship deteriorating or stagnating
- **Stable:** Maintaining current level
- **Growing:** Relationship developing positively
- **Transformative:** Relationship catalyzing significant growth

**Values Alignment:**
- **No:** Fundamental misalignment on core values
- **Partial:** Some alignment, some friction
- **Yes:** Strong alignment on what matters most
