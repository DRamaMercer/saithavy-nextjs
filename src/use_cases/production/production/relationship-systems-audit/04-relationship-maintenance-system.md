# Relationship Maintenance System
## Ongoing Practices for Healthy Connections

**Welcome to your relationship maintenance system.** I used to think relationships either "worked" or "didn't work" - like a light switch, on or off. I didn't understand that relationships are living systems requiring ongoing attention, like a garden or a practice.

I'd go months without connecting with people I claimed mattered to me. I'd neglect relationships until they became fragile, then wonder why they felt strained. I'd wait for crisis to have honest conversations.

This system changed that. Now I have practices, not intentions. Rhythms, not reactions. Maintenance, not just emergencies.

## Part 1: Weekly Relationship Check-ins

A 15-minute weekly practice to stay connected with what matters.

### The Friday 15

**Every Friday, spend 15 minutes reviewing:**

**1. Who did I connect with this week?**

(Not just meetings - real connection)

**2. Which relationships felt energizing?**

**3. Which relationships felt draining?**

**4. What relationship needs attention next week?**

**5. What boundary do I need to maintain or establish?**

## Part 2: Monthly Connection Reviews

A 30-minute monthly practice to assess relationship health and plan intentional connection.

### The Monthly Relationship Review

**At the start of each month, ask:**

**1. Which Tier 1 relationships did I neglect last month?**

(Your 5-7 core people - these cannot be neglected)

**2. Which relationships are drifting?**

(Friends or colleagues you haven't connected with in 30+ days)

**3. Which relationships feel strained?**

(Unresolved tension, unclear expectations, misalignment)

**4. Which relationships need honest conversation?**

(Issues you've been avoiding)

**5. Which relationships am I ready to release?**

(Connections that no longer serve you or align with your values)

## Part 3: Quarterly Relationship Health Assessment

A 90-minute quarterly deep dive into your relationship ecosystem.

### The Quarterly Relationship Audit

**Every quarter (Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct), assess:**

**1. Energy Balance:**
- Current ratio of energy givers to drains
- Time investment by relationship category
- Which relationships are fueling vs. depleting me

**2. Growth & Alignment:**
- Which relationships are growing?
- Which are stagnating or declining?
- Which align with my values? Which don't?

**3. Communication Quality:**
- Am I showing up as I intend to?
- Where are my communication gaps?
- Which relationships need honest conversation?

**4. Boundary Effectiveness:**
- Where am I over-giving?
- Where am I under-investing?
- Which boundaries need strengthening?

**5. Relationship Portfolio:**
- Are my Tier 1, 2, 3 categories still accurate?
- Who should move between tiers?
- Who am I ready to release?

## Part 4: Annual Relationship Deep Dive

A 3-hour annual reflection on your relationship journey.

### The Annual Relationship Review

**Every year (birthday or new year), reflect:**

**1. This Year's Relationship Story:**
- What were my biggest relationship successes?
- What were my biggest relationship failures?
- What did I learn about myself in relationships?
- Which relationships transformed?
- Which relationships ended?

**2. Relationship Identity Evolution:**
- How have I changed as a friend, partner, leader, family member?
- What relationship patterns am I proud of breaking?
- What new patterns am I building?
- Who am I becoming in relationship to others?

**3. Gratitude & Grief:**
- Which relationships am I deeply grateful for?
- Which relationship losses am I still processing?
- Who showed up for me this year?
- Who did I show up for?

**4. Next Year's Relationship Intentions:**
- What kind of friend, partner, leader, family member do I want to be?
- Which relationships do I want to deepen?
- Which relationships do I want to release?
- What relationship boundaries will I strengthen?
- What relationship risks will I take?

**5. Relationship Legacy:**
- How do I want to be remembered by the people in my life?
- What impact do I want to have on others?
- What relationship energy do I want to put into the world?

## Part 5: Daily Relationship Rituals

Small daily practices that maintain connection.

### Daily Connection Practice (5 minutes)

**Every morning or evening:**

**1. Who needs to hear from me today?**
(Text, call, email - just reach out)

**2. Who am I grateful for today?**
(Send a note of appreciation)

**3. Which relationship needs energy today?**
(Set aside time for meaningful connection)

### Daily Micro-Connections

**10-second practices:**
- Send a "thinking of you" text
- Share something that made you think of them
- Express appreciation for something specific
- Acknowledge something you noticed

**1-minute practices:**
- Send a voice message instead of text
- Share a quick update about your life
- Ask how they are and really listen to the answer
- Forward something with a personal note

**5-minute practices:**
- Make a quick call to say hello
- Write a thoughtful message
- Schedule time to connect
- Reflect on a recent conversation

## Part 6: Relationship Boundaries Framework

Boundaries aren't walls - they're the conditions for healthy connection.

### Boundary Categories

**Time Boundaries:**
- Hours I'm available for calls/meetings
- Response time expectations
- When I won't be available
- How much time I'll invest in each relationship tier

**Energy Boundaries:**
- What conversations I won't engage in
- What emotional labor I won't carry
- When I'll step back from draining dynamics
- What I won't absorb from others

**Communication Boundaries:**
- How I want to be communicated with
- What topics are off-limits
- How I'll give and receive feedback
- How I'll navigate conflict

**Value Boundaries:**
- What behavior I won't accept
- What misalignments I won't compromise on
- What I won't participate in
- What I'll speak up about
