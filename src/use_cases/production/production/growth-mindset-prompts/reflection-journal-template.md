# Growth Mindset Reflection Journal

*Your daily companion for the 30-day growth mindset journey*

---

## How to Use This Journal

**Daily Practice:**
1. **Morning** (5 minutes): Read the day's prompt and write your reflection
2. **Midday** (1 minute): Notice your mindset in the moment
3. **Evening** (5 minutes): Integrate what you learned
4. **Weekly** (15 minutes): Review your week and set intentions

**Tips:**
- Be honest with yourself—this is for your eyes only
- Don't worry about perfect grammar or deep insights
- Showing up is more important than what you write
- Date each entry to track your journey over time
