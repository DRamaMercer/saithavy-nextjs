**What "success rate" means:**
- 90%+ = Works excellently with minimal iteration
- 80-89% = Works well with 1-2 iterations
- 70-79% = Works with 2-3 iterations or for most users
- 60-69% = Works but requires skill/iteration (inherently complex tasks)

## Best Practices

### DO:
- ✅ Provide rich context (who, what, why, constraints)
- ✅ Iterate 2-3 times before accepting output
- ✅ Test critical prompts before deploying
- ✅ Save successful prompts for reuse
- ✅ Verify factual claims (AI can hallucinate)
- ✅ Use AI to augment, not replace, human judgment

### DON'T:
- ❌ Accept first draft as final
- ❌ Use vague, generic prompts
- ❌ Trust AI without verification
- ❌ Over-rely on AI for critical decisions
- ❌ Ignore context and constraints
- ❌ Expect AI to read your mind
