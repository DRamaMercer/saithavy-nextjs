# Weekly Intelligence Brief Template

*Structured format for tracking platform algorithm updates, emerging trends, competitive intelligence, and action items across the Swarm Agency network.*

---

## Purpose

This template provides a weekly structure for:

1. **Platform Algorithm Updates** - Track changes across all platforms
2. **Emerging Trends** - Identify and document new content trends
3. **Competitive Intelligence** - Monitor what's working for competitors
4. **Action Items by Swarm** - Assign and track implementation tasks
5. **Performance Review** - Analyze what worked and what didn't
6. **Strategy Adjustment** - Pivot based on intelligence gathered

## Appendices

### A. Glossary

- **Engagement Rate**: ((likes + comments + shares) / followers) × 100
- **CTR**: Click-Through Rate
- **Watch Time**: Total minutes watched
- **Completion Rate**: Percentage of video watched

### B. Platform-Specific Metrics

**TikTok Metrics:**
- Views: Total video plays
- Shares: Number of times shared
- Comments: Number of comments
- Likes: Number of likes
- Saves: Number of times saved

**Instagram Metrics:**
- Reach: Unique accounts reached
- Impressions: Total times content displayed
- Engagement Rate: (interactions / reach) × 100
- Profile Visits: Number of profile visits
- Website Taps: Number of link clicks

**YouTube Metrics:**
- Views: Total video plays
- Watch Time: Total minutes watched
- CTR: Click-through rate (impressions to views)
- Average View Duration: Average time watched
- Subscribers Gained: New subscribers from video
