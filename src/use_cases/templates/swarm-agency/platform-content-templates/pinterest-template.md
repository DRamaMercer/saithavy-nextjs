## Format Specifications

### Standard Pins

**Technical Specs:**
- **Image Ratio**: 2:3 (recommended), 1:1 (square), or 9:16 (vertical)
- **Image Resolution**: 1000x1500 minimum (2:3 ratio)
- **File Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 10MB (recommended: under 2MB)
- **Title**: 100 characters maximum
- **Description**: 500 characters maximum

**Best Practices:**
- Use vertical images (2:3 ratio) for better visibility
- High contrast colors (stand out in feed)
- Clear, readable text overlays (optional)
- Professional quality images

### Idea Pins (Formerly Story Pins)

**Technical Specs:**
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical only)
- **Duration**: Up to 60 seconds per page
- **Pages**: 1-20 pages (optimal: 5-10)
- **File Format**: Video or image

**Content Types:**
- Step-by-step tutorials
- Before/after transformations
- Tips and tricks
- Inspirational collections
- Product showcases

**Best Practices:**
- First page is critical (hook)
- Use consistent branding
- Include detailed captions
- Add music (optional but recommended)
- Use text overlays for key points

### Video Pins

**Technical Specs:**
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (square), 2:3 (vertical), or 16:9 (horizontal)
- **Resolution**: 720p minimum, 1080p recommended
- **Duration**: 15 seconds to 15 minutes (optimal: 30-90 seconds)
- **File Size**: Up to 2GB
- **File Format**: MP4, MOV, or M4V

**Best Practices:**
- First 3 seconds critical (hook viewers)
- Captions recommended (auto-generated or manual)
- Thumbnail important (first frame or custom)
- Educational or inspirational content
- Clear value proposition

### Carousel Pins

**Technical Specs:**
- **Slides**: 2-5 slides
- **Image Ratio**: 1:1 (square) or 2:3 (vertical)
- **Resolution**: 1260x1260 minimum (1:1)
- **File Format**: JPG or PNG

**When to Use:**
- Step-by-step tutorials
- Before/after comparisons
- Product collections
- List articles
- Multiple angles/views

## SEO Strategy

### Keyword Research

**Pinterest-Specific Research:**
```
1. Use Pinterest search bar
   - Type your topic
   - See auto-suggested keywords
   - Note search volume indicators

2. Check Pinterest Trends
   - pinterest.com/trends
   - See trending searches
   - Filter by category and region

3. Analyze competitors
   - Search your topic
   - See top-performing pins
   - Note keywords in titles/descriptions

4. Use Pinterest Analytics
   - Check what people search to find your pins
   - Top keywords for your account
   - Trending searches in your niche
```

### Keyword Placement

```
Title: {Primary keyword} + {Compelling angle}
Description:
  - First 100 characters: Primary keyword + hook
  - Rest of description: Secondary keywords naturally woven
  - Final sentence: CTA

Hashtags: 1-2 relevant hashtags (optional but helpful)

Board Name: {Keyword-rich board name}

Board Description: {Keywords naturally included}

Board Sections: {Keyword-rich section names}
```

### Keyword Formula

```
Primary Keyword + {Angle} + {Value}

Examples:
- "Productivity Hacks for Entrepreneurs"
- "Morning Routine for Busy Moms"
- "Home Office Ideas for Small Spaces"
- "Marketing Tips for Small Businesses"
```

## Algorithm Optimization

### Key Ranking Signals (2025)

1. **Pin Quality** (Most Important)
   - High-resolution images
   - Professional design
   - Clear text overlays
   - Engaging thumbnails

2. **Engagement Rate**
   - Saves (most important)
   - Clicks (outbound)
   - Close-ups (viewing details)

3. **Keyword Relevance**
   - Title keywords
   - Description keywords
   - Board keywords
   - Hashtag relevance

4. **Freshness**
   - New pins get boost
   - Consistent posting rewarded
   - Seasonal content favored

5. **Domain Authority**
   - Quality of destination site
   - Engagement on destination
   - Site load speed
   - Mobile-friendliness

### Content Categorization

```
Help Pinterest categorize your content:

1. Use consistent keywords (titles, descriptions, boards)
2. Create themed boards (not random collections)
3. Pin to relevant boards only
4. Use accurate descriptions
5. Choose correct category for each board
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Low-quality images** - Pinterest is visual-first
2. **Wrong aspect ratio** - 2:3 is optimal
3. **Poor SEO** - Keywords are critical for discovery
4. **No boards** - Pins need board context
5. **Inconsistent posting** - Algorithm rewards consistency
6. **Only self-promoting** - Curate content from others too
7. **Ignoring engagement** - Pinterest is a social platform
8. **Not updating old pins** - Refresh underperforming pins
