## Format Specifications

### Feed Posts

**Character Limits:**
- **Text**: 63,206 characters maximum
- **Optimal Length**: 40-80 characters (for maximum engagement)
- **Link Preview**: Automatically generated (can edit)

**Media:**
- **Images**: Up to 10 photos (1,200x630 minimum recommended)
- **Videos**: Up to 240 minutes, 4GB maximum
- **Links**: Open Graph scraping for preview

**When to Use:**
- Community announcements
- Link sharing
- Photo albums
- Video content
- Polls

### Facebook Stories

**Technical Specs:**
- **Duration**: Each story up to 20 seconds (clips for longer)
- **File Format**: JPG, PNG, MP4
- **File Size**: Under 30MB per story
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (1080x1920)

**Features:**
- Polls
- Questions
- Quizzes
- Links (swipe up for pages with 10K+ followers)
- Music
- Filters and effects

**Best Practices:**
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Daily updates
- Interactive elements (polls, questions)
- Limited-time offers (FOMO)

### Facebook Reels

**Technical Specs:**
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical)
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 recommended
- **Duration**: 15-60 seconds (optimal: 30-45 seconds)
- **File Format**: MP4 or MOV

**Content Strategy:**
- Similar to Instagram Reels/TikTok
- Entertaining or educational content
- Trending audio (when relevant)
- Quick tips and hacks

### Live Video

**Technical Specs:**
- **Duration**: Up to 8 hours
- **Resolution**: 720p minimum, 1080p recommended
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (landscape) or 9:16 (portrait)

**When to Use:**
- Events and conferences
- Q&A sessions
- Behind-the-scenes tours
- Product launches
- Training and education

**Best Practices:**
- Promote in advance (create event)
- Start with hook and overview
- Engage with comments during live
- Have co-host or moderator manage comments
- End with clear CTA
- Save video for later viewing

### Events

**Event Structure:**
```
Event Name: Clear, compelling title
Date & Time: Specific date and time
Location: Physical address or online event link
Description: What, why, who should attend
Cover Photo: 1200x628 recommended
```

**Event Promotion Strategy:**
```
4 weeks out: Create event, initial announcement
3 weeks out: Speaker/feature reveals
2 weeks out: Daily posts, countdown
1 week out: Final push, reminders
Day of: Live updates, excitement
Post-event: Thank you, highlights, recap
```

## Posting Strategy

### Optimal Posting Times (US EST)

```
Thursday: 1-4 PM
Friday: 1-4 PM
Saturday: 12-2 PM
Sunday: 12-2 PM
Wednesday: 12-3 PM
Tuesday: 12-3 PM
Monday: Avoid (busy after weekend)
```

**Note**: Facebook's older demographic (35+) is most active during weekday afternoons and weekends.

### Posting Frequency

```
Page Posts:
  Minimum: 3-5 posts per week
  Optimal: 5-7 posts per week (daily)
  Maximum: 2-3 posts per day (diminishing returns)

Stories:
  Minimum: 3-5 stories per week
  Optimal: Daily (5-10 stories)
  Maximum: 15+ stories per day

Reels:
  Minimum: 2-3 per week
  Optimal: 4-5 per week
  Maximum: Daily

Group Posts:
  Minimum: 3-5 posts per week
  Optimal: Daily (engagement posts)
  Maximum: 2-3 posts per day
```

## Algorithm Optimization

### Key Ranking Signals (2025)

1. **Engagement Rate** (Most Important)
   - Comments > Shares > Reactions
   - Meaningful conversations (not just emoji reactions)
   - Response time to comments

2. **Content Recency**
   - Posts have 4-6 hour lifespan
   - Post when audience is active
   - Consistency rewarded

3. **Content Type Performance**
   - Live Video > Reels > Video > Images > Links
   - Native uploads perform better than shared links
   - Original content preferred

4. **Relationship Building**
   - Engage with your audience
   - Reply to comments quickly
   - Build community connections

5. **Authenticity**
   - Genuine interactions preferred
   - Overly promotional content penalized
   - Community-focused content favored

### Content Categorization

```
Help Facebook categorize your content:

1. Post consistently on specific topics
2. Stay in your niche/area of expertise
3. Engage with related pages and groups
4. Use relevant keywords (in posts, not hashtags)
5. Build community around your brand
```

## Hashtag Strategy

### Facebook Hashtag Formula (1-3 per post)

```markdown
1. **Broad Category** (1 hashtag, optional)
   - Example: #marketing, #business, #community

2. **Specific Topic** (1 hashtag)
   - Example: #digitalmarketing, #smallbusiness, #eventplanning

3. **Branded** (1 hashtag, optional)
   - Example: #yourbrand, #yourcampaign

Total: 1-3 hashtags per post maximum
```

**Note:** Facebook uses hashtags less than Instagram or Twitter. Focus more on keywords in your post text.

## Quality Checklist

### Before Publishing Feed Post

- [ ] Post is 40-80 characters (optimal) or longer if valuable
- [ ] First line grabs attention
- [ ] Clear CTA (if applicable)
- [ ] 1-3 relevant hashtags (optional)
- [ ] Images/videos are high quality
- [ ] Link preview tested (if applicable)
- [ ] Tagged relevant pages/people
- [ ] Location tagged (if relevant)
- [ ] Proofread for errors

### Before Publishing Story

- [ ] Story is 9:16 ratio
- [ ] Text is readable (40pt+)
- [ ] Interactive element included (poll, question, etc.)
- [ ] Branding consistent
- [ ] CTA is clear
- [ ] Sound/music added (optional but recommended)

### Before Publishing Live

- [ ] Promoted in advance
- [ ] Stable internet connection
- [ ] Good lighting and audio
- [ ] Outline or script prepared
- [ ] Moderator assigned (for comments)
- [ ] CTA planned for end of live
- [ ] Test equipment before going live

## Performance Benchmarks

### Good Engagement Rates (by page size)

```
Under 10K likes:
  Post Engagement Rate: 3-5%
  Comment Rate: 1-2%
  Share Rate: 0.5-1%

10K-100K likes:
  Post Engagement Rate: 2-4%
  Comment Rate: 0.5-1%
  Share Rate: 0.3-0.5%

100K+ likes:
  Post Engagement Rate: 1-2%
  Comment Rate: 0.3-0.5%
  Share Rate: 0.1-0.3%

Engagement Rate Formula:
(reactions + comments + shares / reach) × 100
```

### Viral Content Indicators

```
Post:
  Great: 10%+ engagement rate
  Viral: 20%+ engagement rate
  Super Viral: 50%+ engagement rate

Video/Reel:
  Great: 1K+ views
  Viral: 10K+ views
  Super Viral: 100K+ views

Event:
  Great: 100+ responses
  Viral: 500+ responses
  Super Viral: 1,000+ responses
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Over-posting links** - Native content performs better
2. **Ignoring comments** - Engagement is everything on Facebook
3. **Being too promotional** - Provide value first, promote second
4. **Inconsistent posting** - Algorithm and audience punishment
5. **Not using video** - Video outperforms images and text
6. **Ignoring groups** - Groups are where community happens
7. **Poor event promotion** - Events need consistent promotion
8. **Not responding to messages** - Quick responses build trust
