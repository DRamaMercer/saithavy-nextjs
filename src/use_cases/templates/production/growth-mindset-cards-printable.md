## PRINTING INSTRUCTIONS
## ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

**Option 1: Business Card Size**
- Print on cardstock (65-80 lb)
- Cut to 3.5" x 2" dimensions
- Keep in wallet, desk, or bag for daily use

**Option 2: Full Page Size**
- Print single-sided on standard paper
- Place in a binder or folder
- Use as daily journaling prompts

**Option 3: Digital Use**
- Save to phone for quick reference
- Screenshot a daily card
- Set as phone wallpaper for reminder

---

**Every season is training. The point is to keep learning.**
