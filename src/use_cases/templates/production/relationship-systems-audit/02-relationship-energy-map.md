# Relationship Energy Map
## Understanding Your Connection Ecosystem

**Welcome to your relationship energy map.** I used to think all relationships required "work" - and by work, I meant sacrifice. I'd drain myself for people who wouldn't show up for me, then have nothing left for the people who fueled me.

This map changed everything. Some relationships give you energy. Some take energy. Most are neutral. The key isn't to eliminate all energy drains (that's impossible). The key is awareness, so you can make conscious choices about what you're sustaining.
