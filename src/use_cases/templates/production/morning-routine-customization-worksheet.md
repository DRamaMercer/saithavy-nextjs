## Example: Completed Customization Worksheet

**Here's how this worksheet looks when filled out.**

### Sample: The Busy Professional

**Current Reality**:
- Wake-up: 6:30 AM (naturally)
- Start time: 8:00 AM (work from home)
- Available time: 75 minutes
- Realistic routine: 45 minutes (leaving 30 min buffer)

**Primary Goals**:
1. Productivity (focus better, get more done)
2. Energy (not feel tired by 2 PM)

**Energy Profile**:
- Gradual starter (groggy first 30 min)
- Biggest energy boosters: Exercise, sunlight, coffee
- Biggest energy drains: Checking phone immediately, no movement

**Routine Blueprint**:

**Phase 1: Wake & Activate** (12 minutes)
1. Hydrate (1 min)
2. Natural light (3 min)
3. Light movement (3 min)
4. Coffee (5 min - sips while doing other tasks)

**Phase 2: Mental Work** (13 minutes)
1. Brain dump (3 min)
2. Top 3 priorities (2 min)
3. Meditation (8 min)

**Phase 3: Physical/Nourishment** (20 minutes)
1. Exercise (15 min - HIIT circuit)
2. Stretching (5 min)

**Total time**: 45 minutes

**Habit Stack**:
1. After alarm → Get out of bed immediately
2. After getting up → Drink water
3. After water → Step outside for sunlight
4. After sunlight → 10 jumping jacks
5. After jumping jacks → Pour coffee
6. After coffee → Brain dump
7. After brain dump → Write top 3 priorities
8. After priorities → Meditate for 8 minutes
9. After meditation → Exercise for 15 minutes
10. After exercise → Stretch for 5 minutes

**Fallback Routine** (5 min):
1. Hydrate (1 min)
2. Write top priority (1 min)
3. 10 push-ups (1 min)
4. 3 deep breaths (1 min)
5. Gratitude (1 min)

**Result**: After 30 days, he's consistently completing his routine 5-6 days per week. He feels more focused, energized, and less reactive to stress.
