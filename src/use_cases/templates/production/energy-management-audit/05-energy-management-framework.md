## Principle 3: Recovery Is Productive

**Not wasted time. Essential time.**

### The Energy Truth
I used to believe recovery was what you did AFTER work was done. That's backwards. Recovery is what makes work possible. Without recovery, you're not just less productive - you're incrementally destroying your capacity.

**Recovery Myths I Believed:**
- ☐ "I'll rest when I'm done" (you're never done)
- ☐ "Rest is for lazy people" (rest is for sustained capacity)
- ☐ "I can push through" (you can, but you'll pay)
- ☐ "Recovery is wasted time" (lack of recovery is wasted capacity)

**Recovery Truths I Live By:**
- Recovery is productive, not passive

- Recovery is non-negotiable, not optional
