## Allocate Your Energy Like It Matters (Because It Does)

**Energy truth:** I used to budget my money carefully but spend my energy like it was infinite. Then I burned out. This template changed everything.

---

## The Energy Budget Framework

### Total Energy Available: 100%

You get 100 units of energy per day. No more, no less. How you spend it determines your life.

**The Budget Categories:**

### 1. Fixed Costs (60-70%)
Non-negotiable energy expenditures - these must happen.

### 2. Variable Expenses (15-20%)
Optional commitments and activities.

### 3. Energy Savings (15-20%)
Recovery, rest, play - NOT optional, despite what hustle culture tells you.

## Energy Budget Rules I Live By

**Rule 1: Fixed costs first**
Before I commit to anything new, I ensure fixed costs fit within 60-70%.

**Rule 2: Never budget 100%**
Always leave 15-20% for energy savings. This is non-negotiable.

**Rule 3: Variable expenses are optional**
If I'm over budget, variable expenses go first. Not fixed costs, not recovery.

**Rule 4: Energy debt must be repaid**
When I borrow energy, I create a repayment plan immediately.

**Rule 5: Budgets change seasonally**
My energy budget shifts with life circumstances. I adjust quarterly.
