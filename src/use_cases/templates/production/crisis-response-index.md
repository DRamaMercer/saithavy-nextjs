# Crisis Response Plan Template Package

**Complete Crisis Navigation System**

---

## Package Contents

This template package includes 4 comprehensive templates to help you navigate any crisis with clarity, support, and a structured recovery plan.

---

## 1. Crisis Response Plan (Main Template)

**File:** `crisis-response-plan.md`

**What It Is:** The complete crisis navigation system with everything you need from the first hour through long-term recovery.

**What's Inside:**
- Quick-start severity assessment (1-5 scale)
- Immediate action protocol (first 24-72 hours)
- Stabilization plan (emotional + practical)
- Decision-making framework for high-stress situations
- Communication system (who to tell, what to say, when)
- Resource activation checklist
- Recovery roadmap (30-60-90 days)
- Crisis-specific action plans:
  - Professional crisis (job loss, career disruption)
  - Personal crisis (relationship end, divorce)
  - Health crisis (diagnosis, injury, mental health)
  - Financial crisis (debt, bankruptcy, ruin)
  - Existential crisis (identity, meaning, purpose)
- Troubleshooting crisis complications
- Post-crisis integration protocol
- Crisis prevention for the future

**Best For:** Anyone in crisis who needs a complete, structured system.

**Time to Complete:** 30 minutes to read through, use as reference for ongoing crisis.

## 2. Crisis Severity Assessment Tool

**File:** `crisis-severity-assessment.md`

**What It Is:** A 3-minute assessment tool to determine your crisis level (1-5) and immediate action steps.

**What's Inside:**
- 9-question scoring system (36 points total)
- Severity scale with detailed descriptions:
  - Level 1: Discomfort Zone (9-13 points)
  - Level 2: Challenge Zone (14-18 points)
  - Level 3: Crisis Zone (19-24 points)
  - Level 4: Emergency Zone (25-30 points)
  - Level 5: Catastrophe Zone (31-36 points)
- Immediate action steps for each level
- Special cases (mental health emergency, physical safety, financial emergency)
- Progress tracking worksheet

**Best For:** Quick crisis triage—know where you are and what to do first.

**Time to Complete:** 3 minutes to assess, 5 minutes to review action steps.

## 3. Crisis Communication Templates

**File:** `crisis-communication-templates.md`

**What It Is:** Ready-to-use scripts for telling people about your crisis—what to say, when, and to whom.

**What's Inside:**
- Quick reference guide (who to tell when)
- Inner circle templates:
  - Partner/spouse
  - Parents/family
  - Best friend/close friend
- Support circle templates:
  - Extended family
  - Close colleagues
  - Community members
- Professional circle templates:
  - Employer/manager
  - HR/benefits department
  - Clients/customers
  - Professional network
- Creditor/landlord templates
- Sensitive situation templates (boundaries, privacy)
- Social media templates
- Age-appropriate scripts for children
- Post-crisis update templates
- Communication guidelines (do's and don'ts)

**Best For:** Anyone who needs to tell people about their crisis but doesn't know what to say.

**Time to Complete:** 15 minutes to customize scripts, use as needed.

---

## 4. Crisis Decision-Making Framework

**File:** `crisis-decision-making.md`

**What It Is:** A comprehensive system for making high-stakes decisions under extreme stress.

**What's Inside:**
- Decision speed matrix (NOW, TODAY, THIS WEEK, THIS MONTH)
- The 10-10-10 Rule (10 minutes, 10 months, 10 years)
- Decision matrix for comparing options
- "Good enough" protocol (70% rule)
- Values-based decision framework
- Scenario planning (best/worst/likely case)
- Avoiding decision traps (all-or-nothing, catastrophizing, shoulds)
- Group decision-making process
- Post-decision reflection
- Quick-reference decision flowchart

**Best For:** Anyone facing major decisions during crisis who needs clarity and confidence.

**Time to Complete:** 20 minutes to learn framework, use as ongoing tool.

## How to Use This Package

### If You're in Crisis Right Now

**Step 1: Assess your severity**
- Use **Crisis Severity Assessment Tool** (3 minutes)
- Identify your crisis level (1-5)

**Step 2: Take immediate action**
- Go to the **Crisis Response Plan** (main template)
- Complete Part 1: Immediate Action Protocol
- Follow the steps for your severity level

**Step 3: Communicate with your circle**
- Use **Crisis Communication Templates**
- Tell your inner circle within 24-48 hours
- Tell your support circle within the first week

**Step 4: Make decisions with confidence**
- Use **Crisis Decision-Making Framework**
- Apply the 10-10-10 Rule to major decisions
- Use the Decision Matrix for complex choices

**Step 5: Follow your recovery roadmap**
- Continue using the **Crisis Response Plan**
- Complete 30-day, 60-day, and 90-day plans
- Track your progress and adjust as needed

---

### If You're Preparing for Potential Crisis

**Step 1: Build your foundation**
- Review Part 10: Crisis Prevention in the main template
- Complete the Crisis-Resistant Life checklist
- Identify your crisis team before you need them

**Step 2: Customize communication templates**
- Fill in the blanks in **Crisis Communication Templates**
- Store in accessible location (digital + physical)
- Share with your crisis team

**Step 3: Practice decision-making**
- Learn the **Crisis Decision-Making Framework**
- Apply 10-10-10 Rule to current decisions
- Identify your top 3 values in advance
