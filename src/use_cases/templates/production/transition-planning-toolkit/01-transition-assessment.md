## Part 5: Transition Red Flags

### Check In With Yourself

**Are you experiencing:**

**Physical:**
- [ ] Sleep disruption
- [ ] Fatigue or low energy
- [ ] Changes in appetite
- [ ] Physical tension or pain
- [ ] Lowered immune function

**Emotional:**
- [ ] Mood swings or intense emotions
- [ ] Anxiety or panic
- [ ] Depression or hopelessness
- [ ] Irritability or anger
- [ ] Numbness or disconnection

**Cognitive:**
- [ ] Difficulty concentrating
- [ ] Memory problems
- [ ] Confusion or brain fog
- [ ] Indecision
- [ ] Racing thoughts

**Behavioral:**
- [ ] Withdrawal from people
- [ ] Substance use changes
- [ ] Risky behavior
- [ ] Neglecting responsibilities
- [ ] Loss of interest in activities

**If you checked 3 or more in any category**, you're in the intensive zone of transition. This is normal AND it's important to increase support:

- **Connect with a mental health professional** (therapist, counselor)
- **Lean harder into your support system**
- **Simplify everything possible**
- **Focus on basic needs** (sleep, food, movement)
- **Consider if this transition needs professional support**
