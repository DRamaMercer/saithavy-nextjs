# Content Audit Template

**Innovation isn't about new tools—it's about better questions.** The right question: "What content do I already have that's worth repurposing?"

This template helps you inventory your existing content and identify repurposing opportunities.

---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Fill out one audit sheet per core content piece**
2. **Check all applicable repurposing formats**
3. **Track status and performance metrics**
4. **Review monthly to identify patterns**

Download this template, print copies, or create a digital version in your preferred tool (Notion, Airtable, Google Sheets).

### Content Quality Checklist

**Before repurposing, ensure your core content meets these standards:**

- [ ] Addresses specific audience pain point
- [ ] Offers unique perspective or framework
- [ ] Includes real examples or case studies
- [ ] Has clear, actionable takeaways
- [ ] Aligned with your expertise/brand

**If you checked fewer than 4 boxes, consider improving the core content before repurposing.**

### Key Learnings

**After 30 days, answer these questions:**

**1. Which derivative performed best?**
TikTok/Reel with 67K views. The visual, fast-paced format resonated strongly. LinkedIn drove the highest-quality traffic (most click-throughs to full content).

**2. Which platform drove most traffic to core content?**
LinkedIn (567 clicks), followed by Twitter (342 clicks). TikTok drove views but fewer click-throughs—more awareness than conversion.

**3. What surprised you about the results?**
Instagram carousel save rate (4.2%) was double our average. The visual checklist format clearly resonated. Also, newsletter replies were exceptionally high—this topic clearly struck a nerve.

**4. What would you do differently next time?**
Create the TikTok/Reel earlier (it had viral potential). Prioritize LinkedIn and Twitter over Pinterest (we skipped it and don't regret it). Create more downloadable resources—high conversion rate.

**5. Should this content be repurposed again in 6 months?**
☑ Yes

**Why or why not?**
Strong performance across all formats. High engagement, quality traffic, good conversion. Topic is evergreen. Will refresh with new examples and republish.
