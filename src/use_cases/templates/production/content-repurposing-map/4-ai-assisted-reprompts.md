# AI-Assisted Reprompts

**Innovation isn't about new tools—it's about better questions.** The right prompt transforms AI from a generic content generator into your repurposing assistant.

---

## How to Use These Prompts

### The AI Reality Check
AI accelerates content repurposing, but it doesn't replace human judgment. Use these prompts to:

1. **Generate first drafts quickly** (10x faster than manual)
2. **Overcome blank page paralysis** (AI provides structure)
3. **Explore angles you hadn't considered** (new perspectives)
4. **Batch create multiple formats** (parallel processing)

### Critical: Always Edit
- **AI drafts are starting points, not final products**
- **Edit for your brand voice** (AI sounds generic)
- **Add specific examples** (AI invents generic ones)
- **Fact-check all claims** (AI hallucinates statistics)
- **Adjust for platform nuances** (AI doesn't always get context right)

### The Workflow
1. Copy the appropriate prompt below
2. Paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or your preferred AI tool
3. Replace `[bracketed text]` with your specific details
4. Paste your core content at the end
5. Edit the output for brand voice, accuracy, and platform fit

## Important AI Reminders

### What AI Does Well
✅ Generate first drafts quickly
✅ Overcome blank page paralysis
✅ Suggest structures and frameworks
✅ Repurpose for different formats
✅ Provide starting points for editing

### What AI Does Poorly
❌ Capture your unique voice and tone
❌ Include specific, real examples (invents generic ones)
❌ Understand your audience deeply
❌ Get platform nuances exactly right
❌ Fact-check (hallucinates confidently)

### The Golden Rule
**AI drafts = starting point, not final product. Always edit for:**
- Your brand voice
- Real examples and specifics
- Factual accuracy
- Platform-appropriate tone
- Your audience's language and pain points
